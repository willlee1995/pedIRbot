# HKCH Registration Process for IR Procedures
# 香港兒童醫院介入放射科手術登記流程

## Before Your Appointment 預約前

### Referral Process 轉介流程
Patients are referred to HKCH Interventional Radiology through:
病人可透過以下途徑轉介至香港兒童醫院介入放射科：

1. **Specialist clinics 專科門診** - Your child's doctor will arrange the referral
2. **Other HA hospitals 其他醫管局醫院** - Via inter-hospital referral system
3. **Private referral 私家轉介** - With referral letter from private doctor

### Before the Procedure Day 手術前
- You will receive a phone call from HKCH to confirm your appointment
- 您將收到香港兒童醫院的電話確認預約
- Fasting instructions will be provided
- 會提供禁食指示

## On the Day of Procedure 手術當日

### Outpatient Department (OPD) Registration 門診登記
For day procedures 日間手術:
1. Arrive at [LOCATION] at least [X] minutes before your appointment time
2. Bring your child's HKID or birth certificate
3. Bring any relevant medical records or imaging
4. 請於預約時間前至少 [X] 分鐘到達 [位置]
5. 請攜帶小朋友的身份證或出生證明
6. 請攜帶相關醫療紀錄或影像

### Same-Day Admission (SDA) Registration 即日入院登記
For procedures requiring overnight stay 需要留院的手術:
1. Arrive at [LOCATION] at your scheduled admission time
2. Complete admission paperwork at [LOCATION]
3. You will be assigned a bed in [WARD NAME]
4. 請於預定入院時間到達 [位置]
5. 在 [位置] 完成入院手續
6. 您將獲分配 [病房名稱] 的床位

## What to Bring 需要攜帶的物品

### Documents 文件
- [ ] Hong Kong Identity Card or Birth Certificate 香港身份證或出生證明
- [ ] Hospital appointment letter 醫院預約信
- [ ] Referral letter (if applicable) 轉介信（如適用）
- [ ] Previous imaging on CD/DVD (if applicable) 以往影像光碟（如適用）
- [ ] List of current medications 現時服用的藥物清單

### Personal Items 個人物品
- [ ] Comfort items for your child (favourite toy, blanket) 小朋友的安慰物品
- [ ] Change of clothes 換洗衣物
- [ ] Mobile phone and charger 手機及充電器
- [ ] Light reading material 輕便讀物

### Leave at Home 不要攜帶
- Jewelry and valuables 珠寶及貴重物品
- Large amounts of cash 大量現金
