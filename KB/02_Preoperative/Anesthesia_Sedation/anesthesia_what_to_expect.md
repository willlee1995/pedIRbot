# What to Expect with Anesthesia
# 麻醉須知

**Source 資料來源:** KidsHealth (Nemours)
**Category 類別:** Preoperative Instructions 術前指導
**Language 語言:** Bilingual English/Traditional Chinese 中英雙語

---

## Overview 概述

Anesthesia is medicine given during surgery or medical procedures so that your child doesn't feel pain. Understanding what to expect can help both you and your child feel more prepared.

麻醉是在手術或醫療程序期間使用的藥物，讓孩子不會感到疼痛。了解會發生什麼可以幫助您和孩子做好準備。

---

## Top Things to Know 重要須知

| Topic 主題 | What to Expect 預期情況 |
|------------|-------------------------|
| **Preparation 準備** | Health history review, specific instructions provided 健康病史評估，提供具體指示 |
| **During Procedure 手術期間** | Child will not feel pain, may be completely unconscious 孩子不會感到疼痛，可能完全無意識 |
| **Recovery 康復** | May feel sleepy or sore afterward 之後可能感到睏倦或疼痛 |
| **Safety 安全** | Most children do well without complications 大多數孩子沒有併發症 |

## Types of Anesthesia 麻醉類型

### 1. General Anesthesia 全身麻醉

Your child is completely unconscious ("asleep") and unaware during the surgery.
孩子完全無意識（「睡著」），在手術期間不會知道發生什麼。

**How it's given 給藥方式:**
- Through an IV (intravenous line) 通過靜脈注射
- Inhaled through a mask 通過面罩吸入

**What to know 須知:**
- A breathing tube is often used 通常會使用呼吸管
- An anesthesiologist monitors your child throughout 麻醉科醫生全程監護孩子
- Your child won't remember the procedure 孩子不會記得手術過程

---

### 2. Regional Anesthesia 區域麻醉

Numbs a large area of the body while your child may remain awake or lightly sedated.
麻痺身體較大範圍，孩子可能保持清醒或輕度鎮靜。

| Type 類型 | Use 用途 | Description 描述 |
|-----------|----------|------------------|
| **Epidural 硬膜外麻醉** | Pain during/after surgery 手術期間/之後止痛 | Medication injected near spine 藥物注射在脊椎附近 |
| **Spinal Block 脊髓阻滯** | Lower body procedures 下半身手術 | Common for infants, often safer than general 常用於嬰兒，通常比全身麻醉更安全 |
| **Nerve Block 神經阻滯** | One limb or area 一個肢體或區域 | Injected near specific nerves 注射在特定神經附近 |

> 💡 **For Infants 嬰兒:** Spinal blocks are often preferred for quick procedures like hernia repair as they may be safer than general anesthesia for babies.
> 脊髓阻滯常用於疝氣修補等快速手術，對嬰兒可能比全身麻醉更安全。

---

### 3. Local Anesthesia 局部麻醉

Numbs only a small, specific area of the body.
只麻痺身體的小範圍特定區域。

**How it's given 給藥方式:**
- Injection (shot) 注射
- Spray 噴霧
- Ointment/cream 藥膏/乳霜

**Common uses 常見用途:**
- Stitches 縫合傷口
- Dental work 牙科治療
- Minor skin procedures 小型皮膚手術

## Before the Procedure 手術前

### Pre-operative Assessment 術前評估

The medical team will:
醫療團隊會：

- Review your child's medical history 評估孩子的病史
- Ask about current medications 詢問目前服用的藥物
- Check for allergies (especially to anesthesia) 檢查過敏史（特別是麻醉藥物）
- Review family history of anesthesia reactions 了解家族麻醉反應史
- Perform physical examination 進行身體檢查
- Order tests if needed (blood work, X-rays) 如有需要安排檢查（驗血、X光）

### Fasting Requirements 禁食要求

> ⚠️ **Critical 重要:** Follow fasting instructions exactly. If your child eats or drinks too close to the procedure, it could be dangerous.
>
> 請嚴格遵守禁食指示。如果孩子在手術前不久進食或飲水，可能會有危險。

See the [Fasting Guidelines](../Fasting_Guidelines/fasting_guidelines_pediatric.md) for detailed information.
詳情請參閱[禁食指引](../Fasting_Guidelines/fasting_guidelines_pediatric.md)。

## During the Procedure 手術期間

### What the Team Does 醫療團隊的工作

- Anesthesiologists or nurse anesthetists continuously monitor your child 麻醉科醫生或麻醉護士持續監察孩子
- Vital signs checked throughout (heart rate, oxygen, breathing) 全程監測生命體徵（心率、氧氣、呼吸）
- Steps taken to ease fears before medicine is given 給藥前採取措施減輕孩子恐懼
- Some hospitals allow parents to be present until child falls asleep 部分醫院允許家長陪伴直至孩子入睡

### For Your Child 對孩子而言

- Will not feel any pain 不會感到任何疼痛
- May not remember anything about the procedure 可能不會記得手術過程
- Is carefully watched the entire time 全程受到仔細監護

---

## After the Procedure (Recovery) 手術後（康復）

### In the Recovery Room (PACU) 在復甦室

**PACU = Post-Anesthesia Care Unit 麻醉後護理病房**

- Children are monitored closely as they wake up 孩子甦醒時受到密切監察
- Parents can usually join them in the recovery room 家長通常可以到復甦室陪伴
- Nurses will check vital signs frequently 護士會頻密檢查生命體徵

### Common Side Effects 常見副作用

| Side Effect 副作用 | Description 描述 | Duration 持續時間 |
|--------------------|------------------|-------------------|
| **Sleepiness 睏倦** | May be drowsy for an hour or more 可能睏睡一小時或更長 | Usually resolves within hours 通常數小時內消退 |
| **Nausea 噁心** | Some children feel sick to their stomach 部分孩子會感到胃部不適 | Usually temporary 通常是暫時的 |
| **Confusion 迷糊** | May seem confused or irritable 可能顯得困惑或易怒 | Normal upon waking 甦醒時屬正常現象 |
| **Sore throat 喉嚨痛** | Common if a breathing tube was used 如使用呼吸管則常見 | Usually mild, resolves in 1-2 days 通常輕微，1-2天內消退 |

### Going Home 回家

Once your child:
當孩子：

- Is fully awake 完全清醒
- Pain is under control 疼痛受控
- Can keep down fluids 能保持液體不嘔吐
- Vital signs are stable 生命體徵穩定

You will receive:
您會收到：

- Discharge instructions 出院指示
- Pain medication prescriptions (if needed) 止痛藥處方（如有需要）
- Follow-up appointment information 覆診預約資訊

## When to Call the Doctor 何時聯繫醫生

Contact your doctor or seek medical care if your child has:
如孩子出現以下情況，請聯繫醫生或尋求醫療護理：

| Symptom 症狀 | Action 行動 |
|--------------|-------------|
| Fever higher than 101°F (38.3°C) 發燒高於38.3°C | Call doctor 致電醫生 |
| Bleeding, redness, or pus at procedure site 手術位置出血、發紅或有膿 | Call doctor 致電醫生 |
| Severe pain not helped by prescribed medicine 止痛藥無法控制的劇烈疼痛 | Seek medical care 尋求醫療護理 |
| Cannot take fluids by mouth 無法口服液體 | Call doctor 致電醫生 |
| Persistent vomiting 持續嘔吐 | Call doctor 致電醫生 |

## Questions to Ask 可以詢問的問題

Before your child's procedure, consider asking:
在孩子手術前，可考慮詢問：

1. Will my child be sedated before getting the actual anesthesia?
   孩子在接受正式麻醉前會先鎮靜嗎？

2. How will the anesthesia be given (mask, IV, injection)?
   麻醉如何給予（面罩、靜脈注射、注射）？

3. Can I be with my child until they fall asleep?
   我可以陪伴孩子直到他們入睡嗎？

4. How soon after surgery can they eat or drink?
   手術後多久可以進食或飲水？

5. What pain medication will be available?
   有什麼止痛藥可用？

## Special Considerations 特別考慮

### Young Children (Under Age 3) 幼童（3歲以下）

Research is ongoing regarding the effects of anesthesia on brain development in very young children. If you have concerns:
關於麻醉對幼童腦部發育的影響，研究仍在進行中。如有疑慮：

- Discuss with your healthcare provider 與醫療人員討論
- Ask about alternatives if available 詢問是否有替代方案
- Weigh the benefits of the procedure against any potential risks 權衡手術的益處與潛在風險
