## Q1: What is anesthesia and why does my child need it?
## 問題1：什麼是麻醉？為什麼孩子需要麻醉？

**Answer 答案:**

Anesthesia is medicine given during surgery or medical procedures so your child doesn't feel pain. It may make your child sleepy or completely unconscious depending on the type used. Anesthesia is needed because interventional radiology procedures involve inserting catheters or needles into blood vessels, which would otherwise be painful and require the child to stay very still.

麻醉是在手術或醫療程序期間使用的藥物，讓孩子不會感到疼痛。根據使用的類型，可能會讓孩子睏倦或完全無意識。介入放射治療程序涉及將導管或針插入血管，如果沒有麻醉會造成疼痛，而且需要孩子保持非常靜止。

## Q2: What types of anesthesia are used for children?
## 問題2：兒童使用哪些類型的麻醉？

**Answer 答案:**

There are three main types:

**1. General Anesthesia 全身麻醉:**
- Child is completely unconscious ("asleep")
- Given through IV or inhaled through a mask
- A breathing tube is often used
- Child won't remember the procedure

**2. Regional Anesthesia 區域麻醉:**
- Numbs a large area of the body
- Includes epidurals, spinal blocks, and nerve blocks
- Child may be awake or lightly sedated
- Spinal blocks are often preferred for infants for quick procedures

**3. Local Anesthesia 局部麻醉:**
- Numbs only a small, specific area
- Given as injection, spray, or cream
- Used for minor procedures like stitches

主要有三種類型：

**1. 全身麻醉：**
- 孩子完全無意識（「睡著」）
- 通過靜脈注射或面罩吸入給藥
- 通常會使用呼吸管
- 孩子不會記得手術過程

**2. 區域麻醉：**
- 麻痺身體較大範圍
- 包括硬膜外麻醉、脊髓阻滯和神經阻滯
- 孩子可能保持清醒或輕度鎮靜
- 脊髓阻滯常用於嬰兒的快速手術

**3. 局部麻醉：**
- 只麻痺小範圍特定區域
- 以注射、噴霧或乳霜形式給藥
- 用於縫合等小型手術

## Q3: Is anesthesia safe for children?
## 問題3：麻醉對兒童安全嗎？

**Answer 答案:**

Yes, anesthesia is generally very safe for children. Most children do well with anesthesia without any complications. Your child will be continuously monitored by an anesthesiologist or nurse anesthetist throughout the procedure, checking heart rate, oxygen levels, and breathing.

However, for very young children (under age 3), there is ongoing research about the effects of prolonged or repeated anesthesia on brain development. If your child is very young and you have concerns, discuss with your healthcare provider about the benefits of the procedure versus any potential risks.

是的，麻醉對兒童通常非常安全。大多數孩子接受麻醉後都沒有任何併發症。整個手術過程中，麻醉科醫生或麻醉護士會持續監察孩子，檢查心率、氧氣水平和呼吸。

然而，對於非常年幼的兒童（3歲以下），關於長時間或重複麻醉對腦部發育影響的研究仍在進行中。如果您的孩子很小且有疑慮，請與醫療人員討論手術的益處與任何潛在風險。

## Q4: What happens before the anesthesia is given?
## 問題4：給予麻醉前會發生什麼？

**Answer 答案:**

Before anesthesia, the medical team will:

1. **Review medical history:** Ask about medications, allergies (especially to anesthesia), and family history of anesthesia reactions
2. **Physical examination:** Check your child's overall health
3. **Order tests if needed:** Blood work, X-rays
4. **Confirm fasting:** Make sure your child has followed fasting instructions
5. **Place an IV:** A small needle inserted in hand or arm for medications
6. **Ease fears:** Take steps to help your child feel calm

在給予麻醉之前，醫療團隊會：

1. **評估病史：** 詢問藥物、過敏史（特別是麻醉藥物過敏）和家族麻醉反應史
2. **身體檢查：** 檢查孩子的整體健康狀況
3. **如有需要安排檢查：** 驗血、X光
4. **確認禁食：** 確保孩子已遵守禁食指示
5. **放置靜脈注射管：** 在手或手臂插入小針以輸送藥物
6. **減輕恐懼：** 採取措施幫助孩子感到平靜

## Q5: Can I stay with my child until they fall asleep?
## 問題5：我可以陪伴孩子直到他們入睡嗎？

**Answer 答案:**

This varies by hospital and procedure. Many hospitals allow one parent to be present in the anesthesia area until the child falls asleep. Ask your medical team about their policy for parental presence during anesthesia induction at HKCH.

這因醫院和手術而異。許多醫院允許一位家長在麻醉區域陪伴，直至孩子入睡。請向醫療團隊詢問香港兒童醫院關於麻醉誘導期間家長陪伴的政策。

## Q6: What will my child experience when waking up from anesthesia?
## 問題6：孩子從麻醉中醒來時會有什麼感覺？

**Answer 答案:**

After anesthesia, your child will wake up in the recovery room (PACU - Post-Anesthesia Care Unit). Common experiences include:

| Side Effect 副作用 | Description 描述 | Duration 持續時間 |
|-------------------|------------------|-------------------|
| **Sleepiness 睏倦** | May be drowsy for an hour or more 可能睏睡一小時或更長 | Usually resolves within hours 通常數小時內消退 |
| **Nausea 噁心** | May feel sick to stomach 可能感到胃部不適 | Usually temporary 通常是暫時的 |
| **Confusion 迷糊** | May seem confused or irritable 可能顯得困惑或易怒 | Normal upon waking 甦醒時屬正常現象 |
| **Sore throat 喉嚨痛** | Common if breathing tube was used 如使用呼吸管則常見 | Usually mild, 1-2 days 通常輕微，1-2天 |

Parents can usually join their child in the recovery room.

麻醉後，孩子會在復甦室（PACU - 麻醉後護理病房）醒來。常見的情況包括：

| 副作用 | 描述 | 持續時間 |
|--------|------|----------|
| **睏倦** | 可能睏睡一小時或更長 | 通常數小時內消退 |
| **噁心** | 可能感到胃部不適 | 通常是暫時的 |
| **迷糊** | 可能顯得困惑或易怒 | 甦醒時屬正常現象 |
| **喉嚨痛** | 如使用呼吸管則常見 | 通常輕微，1-2天 |

家長通常可以到復甦室陪伴孩子。

## Q7: When can my child eat and drink after anesthesia?
## 問題7：孩子麻醉後何時可以進食和飲水？

**Answer 答案:**

Your child can usually start with clear liquids (water, diluted juice) soon after waking up, once the medical team confirms they are alert enough to swallow safely. If your child tolerates clear liquids without vomiting, they can gradually progress to light snacks and then regular food.

Specific timing will be provided by your medical team based on the type of procedure and how your child is recovering.

孩子通常可以在醒來後不久開始飲用清澈液體（清水、稀釋果汁），前提是醫療團隊確認他們足夠清醒可以安全吞嚥。如果孩子能接受清澈液體而不嘔吐，可以逐漸進食輕食，然後恢復正常飲食。

具體時間會由醫療團隊根據手術類型和孩子的康復情況提供。

## Q8: When should I call the doctor after my child has anesthesia?
## 問題8：孩子接受麻醉後我何時應該致電醫生？

**Answer 答案:**

Contact your doctor or seek medical care if your child has:

- **Fever** higher than 38.3°C (101°F)
- **Bleeding, redness, or pus** at the procedure site
- **Severe pain** not helped by prescribed medicine
- **Cannot take fluids** by mouth
- **Persistent vomiting** that won't stop
- **Difficulty breathing** or unusual breathing sounds

如孩子出現以下情況，請聯繫醫生或尋求醫療護理：

- **發燒**高於38.3°C
- 手術位置**出血、發紅或有膿**
- 止痛藥無法控制的**劇烈疼痛**
- **無法口服液體**
- **持續嘔吐**不停
- **呼吸困難**或異常呼吸聲

## Q9: Will my child be given sedation before the anesthesia?
## 問題9：孩子在接受麻醉前會先鎮靜嗎？

**Answer 答案:**

Some children are given a sedative medication before being taken to the procedure room to help them feel calm and relaxed. This is decided based on:

- Your child's age
- Their anxiety level
- The type of procedure
- Hospital protocols

Ask your medical team whether pre-sedation will be used for your child.

部分孩子在被送往手術室前會獲得鎮靜藥物，幫助他們感到平靜和放鬆。這取決於：

- 孩子的年齡
- 他們的焦慮程度
- 手術類型
- 醫院程序

請向醫療團隊詢問孩子是否會使用預先鎮靜。

## Q10: My child is very anxious about anesthesia. How can I help?
## 問題10：我的孩子對麻醉非常焦慮。我該如何幫助？

**Answer 答案:**

**Before the procedure 手術前:**
- Explain things honestly in age-appropriate terms 用適合年齡的方式誠實解釋
- Reassure them you'll be nearby 向他們保證您會在附近
- Bring a favorite toy or comfort item 帶上喜愛的玩具或安撫物品
- Stay calm yourself - children pick up on parental anxiety 保持冷靜——孩子會感受到家長的焦慮

**Ask the medical team about 向醫療團隊詢問:**
- Child life specialist support 兒童生活專家支援
- Distraction techniques 分散注意力技巧
- Pre-sedation options 預先鎮靜選項
- Whether you can be present during induction 您是否可以在誘導期間在場
