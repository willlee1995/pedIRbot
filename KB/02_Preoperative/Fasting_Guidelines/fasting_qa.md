# Preoperative Q&A: Fasting Guidelines
# 術前問答：禁食指引

## Q1: Why does my child need to fast before the procedure?
## 問題1：為什麼孩子在手術前需要禁食？

**Answer 答案:**

Fasting before a procedure is critical for your child's safety. When under anesthesia or sedation, your child's normal protective reflexes (like swallowing and coughing) are suppressed. If there is food or liquid in the stomach, the child could vomit and the stomach contents could enter the lungs - this is called aspiration. Aspiration can cause severe pneumonia or even life-threatening complications. Fasting ensures the stomach is empty, making the procedure significantly safer.

術前禁食對孩子的安全至關重要。在麻醉或鎮靜狀態下，孩子的正常保護性反射（如吞嚥和咳嗽）會被抑制。如果胃內有食物或液體，孩子可能會嘔吐，胃內容物可能進入肺部——這稱為吸入性肺炎。吸入性肺炎可導致嚴重肺炎甚至危及生命的併發症。禁食確保胃部清空，令手術更加安全。

## Q2: What is the 2-4-6-8 fasting rule for children?
## 問題2：什麼是兒童的2-4-6-8禁食規則？

**Answer 答案:**

The 2-4-6-8 rule specifies when your child must stop eating and drinking before surgery:

- **8 hours before:** Stop all solid foods (rice, bread, meat, noodles) and heavy meals
- **6 hours before:** Stop formula milk and non-human milk (cow's milk)
- **4 hours before:** Stop breast milk
- **2 hours before:** Stop clear liquids (water, apple juice)
- **0 hours (procedure time):** Nothing at all by mouth

2-4-6-8規則規定孩子在手術前必須停止進食和飲水的時間：

- **術前8小時：** 停止所有固體食物（飯、麵包、肉、麵）和正餐
- **術前6小時：** 停止配方奶和非母乳（牛奶）
- **術前4小時：** 停止母乳
- **術前2小時：** 停止清澈液體（清水、蘋果汁）
- **術前0小時（手術時間）：** 完全禁食

## Q3: What counts as a clear liquid?
## 問題3：什麼算是清澈液體？

**Answer 答案:**

**Clear liquids are those you can see through with no pulp or food particles:**

✅ **Allowed:** Water, Pedialyte (electrolyte drinks), apple juice (no pulp), white grape juice, clear Gatorade/sports drinks, clear broth

❌ **NOT allowed (count as solids or milk):** Milk of any type, orange juice (has pulp), smoothies, soup with food particles, formula milk

**清澈液體是指透明、無果肉或食物顆粒的飲品：**

✅ **可以飲用：** 清水、電解質補充液、蘋果汁（無果肉）、白葡萄汁、透明運動飲品、清湯

❌ **不可飲用（被視為固體或奶類）：** 任何種類的牛奶、橙汁（含果肉）、奶昔、有食物顆粒的湯、配方奶

## Q4: What happens if my child eats or drinks when they shouldn't?
## 問題4：如果孩子在不應進食時進食或飲水會怎樣？

**Answer 答案:**

If fasting guidelines are not followed, the surgery will almost certainly be delayed or cancelled to ensure your child's safety:

- **Ate solid food:** Surgery cancelled or delayed 6-8 hours
- **Chewed gum (spit out):** Surgery delayed 2 hours from when gum was spit out
- **Swallowed gum:** Surgery cancelled or delayed 6 hours
- **Drank milk/formula:** Surgery delayed 6 hours
- **Drank clear liquids too close to surgery:** Surgery delayed 2 hours

如果未遵守禁食指引，手術幾乎肯定會延遲或取消以確保孩子的安全：

- **進食固體食物：** 手術取消或延遲6-8小時
- **咀嚼香口膠（吐出）：** 從吐出時起延遲2小時
- **吞下香口膠：** 手術取消或延遲6小時
- **飲用牛奶/配方奶：** 手術延遲6小時
- **太接近手術時間飲用清澈液體：** 手術延遲2小時

## Q5: My child needs to take medication in the morning. What should I do?
## 問題5：我的孩子早上需要服藥。我應該怎麼辦？

**Answer 答案:**

If your child must take required medication on the morning of surgery:

- Take with only a **small sip of water** (not a full glass)
- Do NOT use applesauce or pudding to help swallow pills
- If your child cannot swallow pills, contact your nurse for alternative instructions
- Some medications may need to be held - your medical team will advise

如果孩子在手術當天早上必須服用藥物：

- 只用**少量清水**送服（不是整杯水）
- 不可用蘋果蓉或布甸幫助吞服藥丸
- 如孩子不能吞服藥丸，請聯繫護士獲取替代指示
- 部分藥物可能需要暫停服用——醫療團隊會提供建議

## Q6: How can I help my hungry child wait until the procedure?
## 問題6：我如何幫助飢餓的孩子等待手術？

**Answer 答案:**

**The night before:**
- Give a good dinner at the normal time
- Explain to your child (age-appropriately) why they cannot eat in the morning

**The morning of:**
- Remove food from sight
- Distract with activities (games, books, toys, videos)
- Offer clear liquids until 2 hours before (if allowed)
- Bring entertainment for the hospital wait

**For infants:**
- Plan the last breast/bottle feed carefully based on fasting times
- Wake baby early if needed to fit in a final feed before the cutoff time

**前一晚：**
- 在正常時間給孩子吃豐富晚餐
- 向孩子解釋（根據年齡適當）為何早上不能進食

**手術當天早上：**
- 移走視線範圍內的食物
- 用活動分散注意力（遊戲、書本、玩具、影片）
- 在術前2小時前可給予清澈液體（如獲准許）
- 帶娛樂用品到醫院等候時用

**嬰兒：**
- 根據禁食時間仔細計劃最後一次餵奶
- 如有需要，提早叫醒嬰兒在截止時間前餵奶

## Q7: Is breast milk considered a clear liquid?
## 問題7：母乳算是清澈液體嗎？

**Answer 答案:**

No, breast milk is NOT a clear liquid. Breast milk contains proteins and fats that take longer to digest than clear liquids. The fasting rule for breast milk is **4 hours before** the procedure (not 2 hours like clear liquids).

不，母乳不是清澈液體。母乳含有蛋白質和脂肪，比清澈液體需要更長時間消化。母乳的禁食規則是**術前4小時**（不是清澈液體的2小時）。

---

## Q8: My baby uses thickened formula. Does this change the fasting time?
## 問題8：我的寶寶使用加稠配方奶。這會改變禁食時間嗎？

**Answer 答案:**

Yes, if formula or breast milk has thickeners or rice cereal added, the fasting time may be **longer than 6 hours**. You should contact your nurse or the medical team for specific instructions for thickened feeds.

是的，如果配方奶或母乳添加了增稠劑或米糊，禁食時間可能**超過6小時**。您應該聯繫護士或醫療團隊，獲取加稠飼料的具體指示。

## Q10: Can my child have candy or chewing gum before surgery?
## 問題10：手術前孩子可以吃糖果或香口膠嗎？

**Answer 答案:**

No. Candy is considered a solid food and must be stopped 8 hours before surgery. Chewing gum is particularly problematic:

- **Chewing gum:** Triggers stomach acid production
- **If caught chewing:** Surgery delayed 2 hours from when gum is spit out
- **If swallowed:** Surgery may be cancelled or delayed 6 hours

This applies even to sugar-free gum.

不可以。糖果被視為固體食物，必須在術前8小時停止。香口膠尤其有問題：

- **咀嚼香口膠：** 會觸發胃酸分泌
- **如被發現正在咀嚼：** 從吐出時起延遲手術2小時
- **如吞下：** 手術可能取消或延遲6小時

這也適用於無糖香口膠。
