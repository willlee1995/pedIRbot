# Pediatric Fasting Guidelines (NPO) Before Procedures
# 兒童手術前禁食指引

**Source 資料來源:** Nationwide Children's Hospital, RadiologyInfo.org
**Category 類別:** Preoperative Instructions 術前指導
**Language 語言:** Bilingual English/Traditional Chinese 中英雙語

---

## Overview 概述

Before any procedure requiring anesthesia or sedation, your child must follow strict fasting (NPO - Nothing by Mouth) guidelines. This is critical for your child's safety.

在任何需要麻醉或鎮靜的手術前，您的孩子必須遵守嚴格的禁食（NPO - 術前禁食）指引。這對孩子的安全至關重要。

---

## The 2-4-6-8 Fasting Rule 2-4-6-8 禁食規則

| Time Before Procedure 手術前時間 | What to Stop 停止進食 | Examples 例子 |
|----------------------------------|------------------------|---------------|
| **8 hours 小時** | Solid foods, heavy meals 固體食物、正餐 | Rice, bread, meat, noodles 飯、麵包、肉、麵 |
| **6 hours 小時** | Formula, non-human milk 配方奶、非母乳 | Cow's milk, formula milk 牛奶、配方奶 |
| **4 hours 小時** | Breast milk 母乳 | Breastfeeding 餵哺母乳 |
| **2 hours 小時** | Clear liquids 清澈液體 | Water, apple juice 清水、蘋果汁 |
| **0 hours (Strict NPO) 嚴格禁食** | Nothing at all 完全禁食 | No food, no drinks 不可進食任何東西 |

---

## What Counts as Clear Liquids? 什麼是清澈液體？

### ✅ Allowed 可以飲用
Clear liquids are those you can see through with no pulp or food particles:
清澈液體是指透明、無果肉或食物顆粒的飲品：

- Water 清水
- Pedialyte 電解質補充液
- Apple juice (no pulp) 蘋果汁（無果肉）
- White grape juice 白葡萄汁
- Clear Gatorade/sports drinks 透明運動飲品
- Clear broth 清湯

### ❌ Not Allowed 不可飲用
These count as solid food or milk:
以下被視為固體食物或奶類：

- Milk (any type) 牛奶（任何種類）
- Orange juice (has pulp) 橙汁（含果肉）
- Smoothies 奶昔
- Soup with particles 有食物顆粒的湯

---

## Why is Fasting Important? 為什麼禁食很重要？

### Aspiration Risk 吸入風險

When your child is under anesthesia, their normal reflexes (swallowing, coughing) are suppressed. If there is food or liquid in the stomach:

當孩子接受麻醉時，他們的正常反射（吞嚥、咳嗽）會被抑壓。如果胃內有食物或液體：

- The child could vomit 孩子可能會嘔吐
- Stomach contents could enter the lungs 胃內容物可能進入肺部
- This is called **aspiration** 這稱為**吸入性肺炎**
- It can cause severe pneumonia or life-threatening complications 可導致嚴重肺炎或危及生命的併發症

> ⚠️ **Important 重要提示:** Fasting ensures the stomach is empty, making the procedure significantly safer.
> 禁食確保胃部清空，令手術更加安全。

---

## What Happens If Guidelines Are Not Followed? 如果未遵守禁食指引會怎樣？

### Procedure Delay or Cancellation 手術延遲或取消

If your child eats or drinks when they shouldn't:
如果孩子在禁食期間進食或飲水：

| Situation 情況 | Consequence 後果 |
|----------------|------------------|
| Ate solid food 進食固體食物 | Surgery cancelled or delayed 6-8 hours 手術取消或延遲6-8小時 |
| Chewed gum (spit out) 咀嚼香口膠（吐出） | Delayed 2 hours from when gum was spit out 延遲2小時 |
| Swallowed gum 吞下香口膠 | Surgery cancelled or delayed 6 hours 手術取消或延遲6小時 |
| Drank milk/formula 飲用牛奶/配方奶 | Delayed 6 hours 延遲6小時 |

## Taking Medications 服用藥物

If your child must take required medication on the morning of surgery:
如果孩子在手術當天早上必須服用藥物：

- Take with only a **small sip of water** 只用**少量清水**送服
- Do NOT use applesauce or pudding 不可用蘋果蓉或布甸
- Contact your nurse if your child cannot swallow pills 如孩子不能吞服藥丸，請聯繫護士

> 📞 If you are unsure about any medication, call the hospital for guidance.
> 如對任何藥物有疑問，請致電醫院查詢。

## Tips for Parents 家長小貼士

### The Night Before 前一晚
- Give a good dinner at the normal time 在正常時間給孩子吃豐富晚餐
- Explain to your child why they cannot eat in the morning 向孩子解釋為何早上不能進食

### The Morning Of 手術當天早上
- Remove food from sight 移走視線範圍內的食物
- Distract with activities (games, books, toys) 用活動分散注意力（遊戲、書本、玩具）
- Offer clear liquids until 2 hours before (if allowed) 在術前2小時前可給予清澈液體

### For Infants 嬰兒
- Plan the last breast/bottle feed carefully 仔細計劃最後一次餵奶時間
- Wake baby early if needed to fit in a feed 如有需要，提早叫醒嬰兒餵奶
