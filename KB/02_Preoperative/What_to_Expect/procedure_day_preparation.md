# Procedure Day: What to Expect
# 手術當天：須知事項

**Source 資料來源:** RadiologyInfo.org, Nationwide Children's Hospital
**Category 類別:** Preoperative Instructions 術前指導
**Language 語言:** Bilingual English/Traditional Chinese 中英雙語

---

## Overview 概述

Knowing what to expect on the day of your child's interventional radiology procedure can help reduce anxiety for both you and your child. This guide covers arrival through discharge.

了解介入放射治療當天的情況，有助減輕您和孩子的焦慮。本指南涵蓋由到達至出院的流程。

|-----------|--------------|
| HKCH appointment letter 香港兒童醫院預約信 | Required for registration 登記時需要 |
| Child's ID document 孩子的身份證明文件 | Identity verification 身份核實 |
| Medical card/insurance documents 醫療卡/保險文件 | If applicable 如適用 |

## Clothing Guidance 衣著指引

### For Your Child 孩子衣著

| Type 類型 | Recommendation 建議 |
|-----------|---------------------|
| **Clothing 衣物** | Loose, comfortable clothes that are easy to remove 寬鬆舒適、容易脫除的衣物 |
| **Shoes 鞋子** | Easy slip-on shoes 容易穿脫的鞋子 |
| **Undergarments 內衣褲** | Simple, no metal parts 簡單款式，無金屬部件 |

### Items to Remove Before Procedure 手術前需移除的物品

Your child will be asked to remove:
孩子需要移除：

- Eyeglasses 眼鏡
- Hearing aids 助聽器
- Dental appliances (retainers, dentures) 牙科器具（牙齒固定器、假牙）
- Jewelry (earrings, necklaces, bracelets) 珠寶（耳環、項鏈、手鏈）
- Hair clips with metal 金屬髮夾
- Any metal objects 任何金屬物品

> 💡 **Tip 小貼士:** Leave jewelry at home to avoid losing items.
> 將珠寶留在家中，以免遺失。

## On Arrival at HKCH 到達香港兒童醫院時

### Step-by-Step 步驟

1. **Registration 登記**
   - Proceed to registration counter 前往登記櫃台
   - Present appointment letter and ID 出示預約信和身份證明文件
   - Confirm contact information 確認聯絡資料

2. **Admission/Check-in 入院/報到**
   - You will be directed to the appropriate area 會指引您到適當區域
   - Nursing staff will greet you 護理人員會迎接您

3. **Pre-procedure Assessment 術前評估**
   - Vital signs checked (temperature, blood pressure, heart rate) 檢查生命體徵（體溫、血壓、心率）
   - Final fasting confirmation 最後確認禁食情況
   - Review of medical history and allergies 覆查病史和過敏史
   - Opportunity to ask questions 提問機會

4. **Changing 更衣**
   - Your child will change into a hospital gown 孩子會換上病人服
   - Personal belongings stored safely 個人物品會安全存放

## Before the Procedure 手術前

### IV Line Placement 靜脈注射管置入

- A small needle will be inserted into your child's hand or arm 小針會插入孩子的手或手臂
- This provides access for fluids and medications 用於輸送液體和藥物
- Numbing cream may be applied first to reduce discomfort 可能先塗上麻醉藥膏以減少不適

> 💡 **For anxious children 對於焦慮的孩子:** Ask about distraction techniques or child life specialist support.
> 可詢問分散注意力的技巧或兒童生活專家的支援。

### Site Preparation 部位準備

Depending on the procedure:
視乎手術類型：

- The insertion site may be shaved 可能需要剃除插入部位的毛髮
- Site will be cleaned with antiseptic solution 部位會以消毒液清潔
- Sterile drapes will be placed 會放置無菌布單

### Local Anesthesia 局部麻醉

- The insertion site will be numbed 插入部位會被麻醉
- Your child may feel a brief sting when the local anesthetic is injected 注射局部麻醉藥時可能會感到短暫刺痛
- After this, the area should be numb 之後該區域應該會麻痺

## During the Procedure 手術期間

### What Your Child May Experience 孩子可能的感受

| Sensation 感覺 | Description 描述 |
|----------------|------------------|
| **Pressure 壓力感** | May feel pressure but not sharp pain 可能感到壓力但非劇痛 |
| **Warmth 溫暖感** | When contrast dye is injected 注射造影劑時 |
| **Slight burning 輕微灼熱感** | Brief feeling when contrast enters bloodstream 造影劑進入血液時的短暫感覺 |
| **No catheter sensation 無導管感覺** | Will not feel the catheter moving inside the body 不會感覺到導管在體內移動 |

### Monitoring 監測

Throughout the procedure:
整個手術過程中：

- Heart rate monitored 監測心率
- Blood pressure checked 檢查血壓
- Oxygen levels tracked 追蹤氧氣水平
- Anesthesia team present if sedation/general anesthesia used 如使用鎮靜劑/全身麻醉，麻醉團隊會在場

### Duration 時間

Procedure times vary depending on complexity. The medical team will provide an estimate. Common durations:
手術時間視乎複雜程度而異。醫療團隊會提供估計時間。常見時間：

- Simple procedures: 30-60 minutes 簡單手術：30-60分鐘
- Complex procedures: 1-3 hours 複雜手術：1-3小時

## After the Procedure 手術後

### Immediate Recovery 即時恢復

After the catheter/needle is removed:
導管/針移除後：

- Pressure applied to the site for 10-20 minutes 在部位施壓10-20分鐘
- This helps stop bleeding 這有助止血
- Your child may need to lie flat for several hours 孩子可能需要平躺數小時

### Monitoring Period 觀察期

| Time 時間 | Activity 活動 |
|-----------|---------------|
| First 1-2 hours 首1-2小時 | Close monitoring in recovery area 在復甦區密切觀察 |
| Vital signs 生命體徵 | Checked regularly 定期檢查 |
| Puncture site 穿刺部位 | Monitored for bleeding/swelling 監察出血/腫脹情況 |

### Signs to Report Immediately 需立即報告的情況

Tell the nurse right away if your child experiences:
如孩子出現以下情況，請立即告知護士：

- Bleeding at the insertion site 插入部位出血
- Swelling 腫脹
- Pain at the site 部位疼痛
- Numbness or tingling in the limb 肢體麻痺或刺痛
- Difficulty breathing 呼吸困難

## Going Home 回家

### Before Discharge 出院前

The team will ensure:
團隊會確保：

- Your child is stable 孩子情況穩定
- Pain is adequately controlled 疼痛得到適當控制
- You understand home care instructions 您了解家居護理指示
- Follow-up appointment is scheduled 已安排覆診預約

### Discharge Instructions 出院指示

You will receive written instructions about:
您會收到以下書面指示：

- Activity restrictions 活動限制
- Wound care 傷口護理
- Pain management 疼痛管理
- Diet 飲食
- When to call the doctor 何時聯繫醫生
- Follow-up appointments 覆診預約

### Resuming Normal Activities 恢復正常活動

| Activity 活動 | Typical Timeline 一般時間表 |
|---------------|----------------------------|
| Normal diet 正常飲食 | Usually immediately 通常可即時恢復 |
| Normal activities 正常活動 | 8-12 hours after procedure 手術後8-12小時 |
| Strenuous activity/sports 劇烈活動/運動 | As directed by doctor 按醫生指示 |
| Bathing 洗澡 | As directed by doctor 按醫生指示 |

## Tips for Parents 家長小貼士

### Managing Your Child's Anxiety 處理孩子的焦慮

- Stay calm - children pick up on parental anxiety 保持冷靜——孩子會感受到家長的焦慮
- Explain things in age-appropriate terms 用適合年齡的方式解釋情況
- Be honest about what may be uncomfortable 誠實地說明可能的不適
- Reassure them that you'll be with them 向他們保證您會陪伴他們
- Bring a favorite toy or comfort item 帶上喜愛的玩具或安撫物品

### Managing Your Own Anxiety 處理您自己的焦慮

- It's normal to feel worried 感到擔心是正常的
- Ask questions - the team is there to help 提問——團隊會提供協助
- Take breaks if needed during waiting 等候時如有需要可休息一下
- Update family members rather than holding stress 與家人分享近況，不要獨自承受壓力
