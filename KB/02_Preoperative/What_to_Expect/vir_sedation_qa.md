# Pre-Procedure Q&A: Vascular IR (VIR) with Sedation
# 術前問答：需鎮靜的血管介入放射治療

**Category 類別:** Preoperative Instructions 術前指導
**Source 來源:** HKCH VIR Appointment Sheet
**Format 格式:** Q&A pairs for RAG retrieval

---

## Q1: What is a VIR procedure and does my child need sedation?
## 問題1：什麼是VIR手術？我的孩子需要鎮靜嗎？

**Answer 答案:**

VIR (Vascular Interventional Radiology) includes procedures such as:
VIR（血管介入放射治療）包括以下手術：

- Embolization 栓塞術
- Sclerotherapy 硬化療法
- Central line placement (PICC, Port) 中央靜脈導管置入（PICC、植入式輸液港）
- Angiography 血管造影

**Sedation is typically required for children** because:
**兒童通常需要鎮靜**因為：
- Children need to stay still during the procedure 孩子在手術期間需要保持靜止
- The procedure may be uncomfortable or lengthy 手術可能不舒適或時間較長
- General anesthesia or deep sedation ensures safety and comfort 全身麻醉或深度鎮靜確保安全和舒適

---

## Q2: What is the Pre-Anaesthesia Clinic (PAC) appointment?
## 問題2：什麼是術前麻醉門診（PAC）預約？

**Answer 答案:**

For VIR procedures requiring sedation, a **Pre-Anaesthesia Clinic (PAC)** appointment will be arranged.
需要鎮靜的VIR手術會安排**術前麻醉門診（PAC）**預約。

**At PAC, the anaesthetist will 在PAC，麻醉科醫生會：**
- Perform a pre-sedation assessment 進行術前鎮靜評估
- Review your child's medical history 審視孩子的病歷
- Explain the fasting instructions 解釋禁食指示
- Discuss the sedation/anesthesia plan 討論鎮靜/麻醉計劃
- Answer your questions 回答您的問題

**PAC is usually scheduled before the procedure day**
PAC通常在手術當天之前安排

---

## Q3: What fasting is required for VIR procedures with sedation?
## 問題3：需要鎮靜的VIR手術需要什麼禁食？

**Answer 答案:**

The anaesthetist at PAC will provide specific fasting instructions. General guidelines (2-4-6-8 rule):
PAC的麻醉科醫生會提供具體禁食指示。一般指引（2-4-6-8規則）：

| Food/Drink 食物/飲品 | Stop Before Procedure 手術前停止 |
|---------------------|--------------------------------|
| Solid food 固體食物 | 8 hours 8小時 |
| Formula/cow's milk 配方奶/牛奶 | 6 hours 6小時 |
| Breast milk 母乳 | 4 hours 4小時 |
| Clear liquids 清澈液體 | 2 hours 2小時 |

**Follow the specific instructions given at PAC** - they are tailored to your child.
**請遵循PAC提供的具體指示** - 這是為您的孩子度身訂製的。

## Q4: What preparation does the ward need to do before VIR?
## 問題4：病房在VIR手術前需要做什麼準備？

**Answer 答案:**

**Ward staff will 病房人員會：**

| Preparation 準備 | Details 詳情 |
|-----------------|--------------|
| Blood tests 驗血 | Check INR (<1.5) and Platelets (>50 x 10⁹/L) within 2 weeks 2周內檢查 |
| Consent form 同意書 | Prepare valid Informed Consent Form 準備有效知情同意書 |
| Site marking 部位標記 | Mark procedure site/side when appropriate 適當時標記手術部位/側 |
| Pregnancy test 懷孕測試 | Exclude pregnancy for females >10 years old 10歲以上女性排除懷孕 |
| IV access 靜脈通道 | Prepare peripheral IV at non-affected side 在非受影響側準備周邊靜脈通道 |

## Q5: How should my child be transported to the IR suite?
## 問題5：孩子應該如何運送到介入放射治療室？

**Answer 答案:**

**For inpatient VIR procedures 住院VIR手術：**

- Patient should wear **hospital gown (開口紫袍)** and **OT cap**
- 病人應穿著**醫院袍（開口紫袍）**和**手術帽**
- Transport on **stretcher** 躺在**擔架床**上運送
- **Nurse escort required** for all cases 所有病例**需要護士陪同**
- **Doctor and nurse escort** required for urgent cases 緊急病例需要**醫生和護士陪同**
- Patient should **empty bladder** before coming to IR suite 到介入放射治療室前應**排空膀胱**

## Q6: Does my child need to stay in hospital after VIR?
## 問題6：孩子在VIR手術後需要留院嗎？

**Answer 答案:**

This depends on:
這取決於：

| Factor 因素 | Typical Plan 一般計劃 |
|------------|---------------------|
| **Type of procedure 手術類型** | Simple procedures may be day cases 簡單手術可能是日間手術 |
| **Sedation level 鎮靜程度** | Deep sedation may require monitoring 深度鎮靜可能需要監測 |
| **Patient's condition 病人狀況** | Complex cases may need admission 複雜病例可能需要入院 |
| **Time of procedure 手術時間** | Late procedures may require overnight stay 較晚的手術可能需要過夜 |

Your appointment letter will indicate if **Admission 入院** is required.
您的預約信會標明是否需要**入院**。

## Q7: What if my child is receiving blood transfusions?
## 問題7：如果孩子正在接受輸血怎麼辦？

**Answer 答案:**

If your child is receiving blood product transfusions:
如果您的孩子正在接受血液製品輸血：

- **A nurse from the referring unit must stay** in the Department of Radiology during the procedure
- **轉介單位的護士必須留在**放射科直到手術完成
- This ensures proper monitoring and care 這確保適當的監測和護理
- Coordinate with your ward before the procedure day 手術當天前與病房協調

---

## Q8: What should I do if my child has diabetes?
## 問題8：如果孩子有糖尿病應該怎麼辦？

**Answer 答案:**

For children with diabetes mellitus on medications:
患有糖尿病並正在服藥的兒童：

- **Consult the paediatrician or nurse** for specific instructions
- **諮詢兒科醫生或護士**以獲取具體指示
- Fasting instructions may need to be adjusted 禁食指示可能需要調整
- Blood sugar monitoring may be needed 可能需要監測血糖
- Inform the IR team on the procedure day 手術當天告知介入放射治療團隊

---

## Q9: What happens during recovery after sedation?
## 問題9：鎮靜後的恢復期間會發生什麼？

**Answer 答案:**

After VIR procedures with sedation:
鎮靜VIR手術後：

**In the recovery area 在恢復區：**
- Vital signs monitored 監測生命體徵
- Wait until child is awake and stable 等待孩子清醒和穩定
- Pain assessed and managed 評估和控制疼痛
- Clear instructions given before discharge 出院前給予清晰指示

**You cannot take your child home until 您不能帶孩子回家直到：**
- Fully awake and alert 完全清醒和警覺
- Eating and drinking 能進食和飲水
- Medical team gives clearance 醫療團隊批准
