## Q3: Does my child need to fast before the IR procedure?
## 問題3：孩子做介入放射治療前需要禁食嗎？

**Answer 答案:**

**It depends on the procedure type 視乎手術類型而定:**

**Procedures requiring sedation/anesthesia 需要鎮靜/麻醉的手術:**
- YES, fasting is required 需要禁食
- A Pre-Anaesthesia Clinic (PAC) appointment will be arranged 會安排術前麻醉門診（PAC）
- The anaesthetist will explain fasting instructions 麻醉科醫生會解釋禁食指示

**Minor procedures without sedation 不需鎮靜的小型手術:**
- Fasting requirements vary 禁食要求不同
- Follow the specific instructions on your appointment sheet 請遵循預約信上的具體指示

## Q4: What preparations are needed before HKCH IR procedures?
## 問題4：香港兒童醫院介入放射治療前需要什麼準備？

**Answer 答案:**

**Pre-procedure requirements 術前要求:**

| Requirement 要求 | Details 詳情 |
|-----------------|--------------|
| **Blood tests 驗血** | INR < 1.5, Platelets > 50 x 10⁹/L (within 2 weeks) |
| **Coagulation check 凝血檢查** | Required within 2 weeks of procedure 手術前2周內需要 |
| **Anti-coagulation 抗凝血藥** | Contact IR at 3513 6100 for special arrangement 請致電3513 6100特別安排 |
| **Contrast allergy 造影劑過敏** | Steroid premedication may be required 可能需要類固醇前期藥物 |
| **Pregnancy 懷孕** | Must be excluded for female patients >10 years 10歲以上女性患者必須排除懷孕 |

## Q5: My child is on blood thinners. What should I do?
## 問題5：我的孩子正在服用薄血藥。我應該怎麼辦？

**Answer 答案:**

If your child is taking anti-coagulation or anti-platelet medications:
如果您的孩子正在服用抗凝血或抗血小板藥物：

1. **Contact the IR team before the procedure** 手術前聯繫介入放射治療團隊
2. Call: 3513 6100 致電：3513 6100
3. Special arrangements may be needed 可能需要特別安排
4. DO NOT stop medications without medical advice 未經醫療建議請勿停藥

The team will advise on when to stop and restart these medications.
團隊會建議何時停藥及重新服藥。

## Q6: What if my child had a previous reaction to contrast dye?
## 問題6：如果我的孩子之前對造影劑有過敏反應怎麼辦？

**Answer 答案:**

If your child has a history of contrast (iodinated) reactions:
如果您的孩子有造影劑（碘化物）過敏反應史：

- **Steroid premedication is required** 需要類固醇預防藥物
- This must be prescribed before the procedure day 必須在手術當天前開具處方
- Inform the referring doctor and IR team 請告知轉介醫生和介入放射治療團隊
- Follow the hospital's steroid premedication guidelines 遵循醫院的類固醇預防藥物指引

## Q7: Does my child need to be admitted for the IR procedure?
## 問題7：孩子做介入放射治療需要入院嗎？

**Answer 答案:**

**Yes, all IR procedures require some form of admission** 是的，所有介入放射治療都需要某種形式的入院：

| Procedure Type 手術類型 | Admission Type 入院類型 |
|------------------------|------------------------|
| **Simple procedures 簡單手術** | ACC (Ambulatory Care Centre) 日間護理中心 |
| **Complex VIR 複雜血管介入** | Ward admission 病房入院 |
| **Procedures requiring sedation 需要鎮靜的手術** | Follow admission instructions 按入院指示 |

**Please follow your specific admission instructions** 請遵循您的具體入院指示：
- Your appointment letter will indicate the type of admission required
- 您的預約信會標明所需的入院類型
- If unsure, contact Radiology at 3513 6100
- 如不確定，請致電放射科 3513 6100

Your appointment letter/sheet will indicate:
您的預約信/單會標明：
- ☐ Admission 入院
- ☐ Sedation 鎮靜程序

## Q8: Who can accompany my child to the IR procedure?
## 問題8：誰可以陪同孩子做介入放射治療？

**Answer 答案:**

**During transport 運送期間:**
- Children must be accompanied by parents/guardian until arrival at the examination room
- 孩子必須由父母/監護人陪同直到抵達檢查室

**For inpatients requiring transport 需要運送的住院病人:**
- Nurse escort is required for all cases to and from Department of Radiology
- 所有病人往返放射科均需護士陪同
- Doctor and nurse escorts are required for urgent cases 緊急病例需要醫生和護士陪同
- Patient should be transported on stretcher wearing hospital gown and OT cap
- 病人應穿著醫院袍和手術帽，躺在擔架床上運送

## Q9: What happens if there is a typhoon or rainstorm on procedure day?
## 問題9：如果手術當天有颱風或暴雨怎麼辦？

**Answer 答案:**

**Typhoon Signal No. 8 or above 八號或以上颱風信號：**
- Appointment will be cancelled 預約將會取消
- Services resume 2 hours after signal lowered 信號取消後2小時恢復服務
- (Unless within 2 hours of closing time) （除非距離關門時間少於2小時）

**Black Rainstorm Warning (outside service hours) 黑色暴雨警告（服務時間外）：**
- Appointment will be cancelled 預約將會取消
- Services resume 2 hours after warning cancelled 警告取消後2小時恢復服務

**Black Rainstorm (during service hours) 黑色暴雨（服務時間內）：**
- Services continue as normal 服務照常進行

The Department will reschedule and inform you as soon as possible.
部門會盡快重新安排並通知您。

## 問題10：如何重新安排或取消孩子的介入放射治療預約？

**Answer 答案:**
