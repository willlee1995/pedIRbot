# Arteriovenous Malformations (AVM)
# 動靜脈畸形

*Source: KidsHealth (Nemours Children's Health)*

---

## What Is an Arteriovenous Malformation? 什麼是動靜脈畸形？

An arteriovenous malformation (AVM) is an abnormal connection between an artery (carrying blood from the heart) and a vein (returning blood to the heart). It bypasses capillaries, which are necessary for nutrient and oxygen exchange.

動靜脈畸形（AVM）是動脈（從心臟輸送血液）和靜脈（將血液送回心臟）之間的異常連接。它繞過毛細血管，而毛細血管是營養和氧氣交換所必需的。

This "shortcut" causes the heart to work harder, which can lead to heart strain or failure in large or multiple AVMs.

這種「捷徑」導致心臟更加努力工作，這可能導致大型或多發性AVM中的心臟負擔或衰竭。

## Signs & Symptoms 徵兆與症狀

### External AVMs 外部AVM
- Pink, red, or purple birthmark 粉紅色、紅色或紫色胎記
- Pain and swelling 疼痛和腫脹
- Bleeding (can be difficult to stop and may lead to anemia) 出血（可能難以止血，可能導致貧血）
- Warmer skin over the area 該區域皮膚較暖
- Palpable pulse (you can feel the blood flow) 可觸及的脈搏（可以感覺到血流）

### Intracranial AVMs (In the Head) 顱內AVM（腦部）
- Headaches 頭痛
- Seizures 癲癇發作
- Vision problems 視力問題
- Confusion 意識混亂
- Trouble with movement or speech 活動或說話困難

## Causes 成因

AVMs are vascular malformations that occur when blood vessels don't develop normally during fetal growth. They are often associated with genetic syndromes:

AVM是血管畸形，當胎兒發育期間血管未正常發展時發生。它們通常與遺傳綜合症相關：

| Syndrome 綜合症 | Characteristics 特徵 |
|----------------|---------------------|
| **Cobb syndrome** | Skin/spine AVMs 皮膚/脊椎AVM |
| **HHT (Hereditary Hemorrhagic Telangiectasia)** 遺傳性出血性毛細血管擴張症 | Lung, brain, and GI tract AVMs 肺部、腦部和腸胃道AVM |
| **Parkes Weber syndrome** | Multiple AVMs in a limb causing overgrowth 肢體多發AVM導致過度生長 |
| **Wyburn-Mason syndrome** | Retina and brain AVMs 視網膜和腦部AVM |

## Diagnosis 診斷

### Physical Exam 身體檢查
Feeling a pulse in the vessels.
在血管中觸及脈搏。

### Imaging Studies 影像檢查

| Test 檢查 | Purpose 用途 |
|----------|-------------|
| **Ultrasound 超聲波** | Maps blood flow patterns 繪製血流模式 |
| **CT Scan 電腦斷層掃描** | Determines size and proximity to organs 確定大小和與器官的接近程度 |
| **MRI 磁力共振成像** | Detailed soft tissue imaging 詳細軟組織成像 |
| **Angiogram 血管造影** | Maps blood vessels to plan treatment 繪製血管地圖以規劃治療 |

> **Note:** Angiograms can be performed as standard (catheter-based), CT angiography, or MR angiography.
> **注意：** 血管造影可以作為標準（導管式）、CT血管造影或MR血管造影進行。

## Treatment Options 治療選擇

Treatment depends on location and size. AVMs outside the skull are typically treated by **Interventional Radiologists**.

治療取決於位置和大小。顱外AVM通常由**介入放射科醫生**治療。

### Embolization 栓塞術
A catheter is used to block the feeder arteries with synthetic materials.
使用導管用合成材料阻斷供血動脈。

**This is a primary Interventional Radiology treatment.**
**這是介入放射科的主要治療方法。**

### Sclerotherapy 硬化療法
Injection of medicine to shrink the vessels.
注射藥物以縮小血管。

### Surgery 手術
Removal of the malformation.
切除畸形。

### Intracranial AVMs 顱內AVM治療
May require:
可能需要：
- Surgery 手術
- Embolization 栓塞術
- Radiosurgery (focused radiation) 放射手術（聚焦放射線）

## Important Considerations 重要注意事項

> [!WARNING]
> AVMs can cause serious bleeding if injured. If you or your child has an AVM, inform all healthcare providers about this condition before any procedures.
> AVM如果受傷可能會導致嚴重出血。如果您或您的孩子有AVM，在任何程序之前請告知所有醫療保健提供者這種情況。

### Emergency Signs 緊急徵兆
Seek immediate medical attention for:
以下情況請立即就醫：
- Sudden severe headache 突然嚴重頭痛
- Uncontrolled bleeding 無法控制的出血
- Sudden weakness or numbness 突然無力或麻木
- Vision changes 視力變化
- Difficulty speaking 說話困難
