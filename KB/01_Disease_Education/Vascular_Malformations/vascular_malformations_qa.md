# 疾病教育問答：血管畸形

## Q1: What is the difference between a hemangioma and a vascular malformation?
## 問題1：血管瘤和血管畸形有什麼區別？

**Answer 答案:**

| Feature 特徵 | Hemangioma 血管瘤 | Vascular Malformation 血管畸形 |
|--------------|-------------------|-------------------------------|
| **When appears 何時出現** | Usually appears shortly after birth 通常在出生後不久出現 | Present at birth (may not be visible) 出生時存在（可能不可見） |
| **Growth pattern 生長模式** | Rapid growth in first year, then shrinks 第一年快速生長，然後縮小 | Grows with the child, doesn't shrink 隨孩子生長，不會縮小 |
| **Nature 性質** | Tumor (abnormal cell growth) 腫瘤（異常細胞生長） | Structural abnormality (abnormal vessels) 結構異常（異常血管） |
| **Resolution 自然消退** | Most resolve by age 5-10 大多數在5-10歲時消退 | Do not resolve on their own 不會自行消退 |

## Q2: What is a hemangioma?
## 問題2：什麼是血管瘤？

**Answer 答案:**

A hemangioma is a benign (non-cancerous) tumor made of extra blood vessels. It's the most common vascular tumor in infants.

**Key facts 主要事實:**
- Usually appears in first few weeks of life 通常在出生後首幾周出現
- More common in girls, premature babies, and twins 女孩、早產兒和雙胞胎更常見
- Goes through growth phase (first year), then involutes (shrinks) 經歷生長期（第一年），然後退化（縮小）
- About 90% resolve by age 10 約90%在10歲時消退
- May need treatment if affecting vision, breathing, or feeding 如影響視力、呼吸或餵食可能需要治療

血管瘤是由額外血管組成的良性（非癌性）腫瘤。這是嬰兒最常見的血管腫瘤。

## Q3: What is a lymphatic malformation?
## 問題3：什麼是淋巴管畸形？

**Answer 答案:**

A lymphatic malformation (also called lymphangioma) is an abnormal collection of lymph vessels and fluid-filled spaces. These are present at birth but may not be noticed until later.

**Types 類型:**
- **Macrocystic 大囊型:** Large fluid-filled cysts, often in neck/armpit 大的充滿液體的囊腫，常在頸部/腋窩
- **Microcystic 小囊型:** Tiny cysts, often affects skin and tongue 微小囊腫，常影響皮膚和舌頭
- **Mixed 混合型:** Combination of both 兩者結合

**Treatment 治療:** Sclerotherapy (injection to shrink) is often the first treatment. Some may need surgery.
硬化療法（注射使其縮小）通常是首選治療。部分可能需要手術。

淋巴管畸形（也稱淋巴管瘤）是淋巴管和充滿液體空間的異常集合。這些在出生時存在，但可能到後來才被發現。

## Q4: What is a venous malformation?
## 問題4：什麼是靜脈畸形？

**Answer 答案:**

A venous malformation (VM) is an abnormal collection of veins that didn't develop properly. The veins are stretched and misshapen, causing blood to pool.

**Characteristics 特徵:**
- Present at birth 出生時存在
- Grows proportionally with child 與孩子成比例生長
- May appear as soft, bluish swelling 可能呈現柔軟、藍色腫脹
- Can swell with activity, crying, or straining 活動、哭泣或用力時可能腫脹
- May cause pain or discomfort 可能導致疼痛或不適

**Treatment 治療:** Sclerotherapy is the primary interventional radiology treatment, often combined with compression therapy.
硬化療法是主要的介入放射治療，通常與壓迫療法結合。

靜脈畸形是發育不正常的靜脈異常集合。靜脈被拉伸和變形，導致血液淤積。

## Q5: What is an AVM (arteriovenous malformation)?
## 問題5：什麼是動靜脈畸形（AVM）？

**Answer 答案:**

An AVM (arteriovenous malformation) is an abnormal tangle of blood vessels where arteries connect directly to veins, bypassing the normal capillary network. This causes high-pressure blood flow.

**Key features 主要特徵:**
- Can occur anywhere in body, including brain 可發生在身體任何部位，包括腦部
- Often warm to touch due to high blood flow 由於高血流量，觸摸時通常溫暖
- May throb or have a pulse 可能搏動或有脈搏
- Can grow rapidly, especially during puberty or pregnancy 可能快速生長，尤其在青春期或懷孕期間
- More complex to treat than other vascular malformations 比其他血管畸形更難治療

**Treatment 治療:** Embolization is often used to block the abnormal vessels. May need multiple treatments.
栓塞術常用於阻塞異常血管。可能需要多次治療。

動靜脈畸形是血管的異常纏結，其中動脈直接連接到靜脈，繞過正常的毛細血管網絡。這導致高壓血流。

## Q6: Will my child need treatment for their vascular malformation?
## 問題6：我的孩子的血管畸形需要治療嗎？

**Answer 答案:**

Not all vascular malformations need treatment. The decision depends on:

**Treatment may be recommended if 如有以下情況可能建議治療:**
- Causing pain or discomfort 導致疼痛或不適
- Affecting function (vision, breathing, eating, walking) 影響功能（視力、呼吸、進食、行走）
- Causing bleeding episodes 導致出血發作
- Causing disfigurement 導致變形
- Growing rapidly 快速生長

**Watchful waiting may be appropriate for 觀察等待可能適用於:**
- Small, asymptomatic malformations 小型、無症狀的畸形
- Hemangiomas (most shrink on their own) 血管瘤（大多數會自行縮小）
- Malformations in areas where treatment risks outweigh benefits 治療風險大於益處的區域的畸形

Your HKCH team will discuss the best approach for your child.
香港兒童醫院團隊會與您討論最適合孩子的方案。

## Q7: What is sclerotherapy and how does it treat vascular malformations?
## 問題7：什麼是硬化療法？它如何治療血管畸形？

**Answer 答案:**

Sclerotherapy is a minimally invasive treatment where a medication (sclerosant) is injected directly into the abnormal vessels to make them shrink and close.

**How it works 如何運作:**
1. Under imaging guidance, a needle is inserted into the malformation 在影像引導下，將針插入畸形
2. A sclerosing agent is injected 注射硬化劑
3. The sclerosant irritates the vessel lining 硬化劑刺激血管內膜
4. This causes the vessels to scar closed over time 這導致血管隨時間疤痕閉合

**What to expect 預期情況:**
- Usually done under general anesthesia for children 兒童通常在全身麻醉下進行
- Outpatient procedure (go home same day) 門診手術（當天回家）
- Swelling and bruising expected for 1-2 weeks 預計腫脹和瘀青1-2周
- Often requires multiple sessions 通常需要多次療程

硬化療法是一種微創治療，將藥物（硬化劑）直接注射到異常血管中，使它們縮小和閉合。

## Q8: What is embolization?
## 問題8：什麼是栓塞術？

**Answer 答案:**

Embolization is a minimally invasive procedure where materials are injected through a catheter to block abnormal blood vessels.

**How it works 如何運作:**
1. A catheter is inserted through a blood vessel (usually in groin or arm) 通過血管插入導管（通常在腹股溝或手臂）
2. Using X-ray guidance, the catheter is navigated to the abnormal vessels 使用X光引導，將導管導航到異常血管
3. Embolic materials (coils, particles, glue, or liquid agents) are injected 注射栓塞物質（線圈、顆粒、膠水或液體製劑）
4. This blocks blood flow to the abnormal area 這阻斷了流向異常區域的血流

**Used for 用於:**
- AVMs (arteriovenous malformations) 動靜脈畸形
- Some tumors 部分腫瘤
- Bleeding control 出血控制

栓塞術是一種微創手術，通過導管注射物質來阻塞異常血管。

## Q9: Will my child's vascular malformation come back after treatment?
## 問題9：我孩子的血管畸形治療後會復發嗎？

**Answer 答案:**

This depends on the type of malformation and treatment:

**Hemangiomas 血管瘤:**
- Usually do not recur after spontaneous resolution or treatment 自然消退或治療後通常不會復發

**Lymphatic/Venous Malformations 淋巴管/靜脈畸形:**
- May require multiple treatment sessions 可能需要多次治療療程
- Some residual malformation often remains 通常會有一些殘留畸形
- Can regrow if not completely treated 如未完全治療可能再生
- Long-term follow-up is important 長期隨訪很重要

**AVMs 動靜脈畸形:**
- More complex, higher recurrence rates 更複雜，復發率更高
- Often require ongoing monitoring and multiple treatments 通常需要持續監測和多次治療
- May need lifelong management 可能需要終身管理

## Q10: Are vascular malformations hereditary? Will my other children have them?
## 問題10：血管畸形是遺傳的嗎？我的其他孩子會有嗎？

**Answer 答案:**

**Most vascular malformations are NOT hereditary** - they occur sporadically (randomly) and are not passed from parent to child.

**Exceptions 例外情況:**
- Some syndromic conditions that include vascular malformations can be hereditary (e.g., hereditary hemorrhagic telangiectasia/HHT, some forms of VM)
- If your child has a syndrome associated with vascular malformations, genetic counseling may be recommended

If you have concerns about heredity, discuss this with your HKCH medical team who can advise on genetic testing if appropriate.

**大多數血管畸形不是遺傳性的** - 它們是偶發性（隨機）發生的，不會從父母傳給孩子。

**例外情況：**
- 一些包括血管畸形的綜合症可能是遺傳性的（例如遺傳性出血性毛細血管擴張症/HHT，某些形式的靜脈畸形）
- 如果您的孩子有與血管畸形相關的綜合症，可能建議遺傳諮詢

如有遺傳方面的疑慮，請與香港兒童醫院醫療團隊討論，他們可在適當時建議遺傳測試。
