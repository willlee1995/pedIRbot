# Venous Malformations
# 靜脈畸形

*Source: Boston Children's Hospital*

---

## Overview 概述

A venous malformation (VM) is a lesion caused by abnormally shaped veins that can stretch and enlarge as a child grows. It is the most common type of vascular malformation.

靜脈畸形（VM）是由形狀異常的靜脈引起的病變，隨著兒童成長可能會拉伸和擴大。這是最常見的血管畸形類型。

### Rare Sub-types 罕見亞型
- **Glomuvenous malformation (GVM)** 球靜脈畸形
- **Cerebral-cavernous malformation (CCM)** 腦海綿狀畸形
- **Blue rubber bleb nevus syndrome (BRBNS)** 藍色橡皮疱痣綜合症
- **Maffucci syndrome** 馬富奇綜合症

---

## Symptoms 症狀

- Typically appear as blue, soft, compressible lesions on the skin or internal organs
  通常表現為皮膚或內臟器官上的藍色、柔軟、可壓縮的病變

- Pain 疼痛
- Swelling 腫脹
- Potential obstruction of vital functions 可能阻礙重要功能

---

## Causes 成因

- Caused by genetic mutations during embryonic development
  由胚胎發育過程中的基因突變引起

- **NOT caused by any maternal actions during pregnancy**
  **不是由懷孕期間母親的任何行為引起**

---

## Potential Complications 潛在併發症

- Sudden expansion due to blood clots 由於血栓導致突然擴大
- Fractures if bone is affected 如果骨骼受影響可能骨折
- Pulmonary embolism (rare) 肺栓塞（罕見）
- Chronic bleeding and anemia in gastrointestinal cases 胃腸道病例中的慢性出血和貧血

---

## Diagnosis 診斷

### Primary Assessment 主要評估
- Medical history 病史
- Physical examination 身體檢查

### Imaging Studies 影像檢查
- **Ultrasound** 超聲波 - Initial imaging
- **MRI** 磁力共振成像 - To determine size and extent
- **CT scan** 電腦斷層掃描 - When needed

---

## Treatment Options 治療選擇

> **Important:** There is no cure for venous malformations. Treatment focuses on symptom management.
>
> **重要：** 靜脈畸形無法治癒。治療重點是症狀管理。

### Sclerotherapy 硬化療法
The primary treatment where a solution is injected to shrink abnormal veins.
主要治療方法，注射溶液以縮小異常靜脈。

**This is a common Interventional Radiology procedure.**
**這是介入放射科的常見程序。**

### Compression Garments 壓力衣
To reduce pain and swelling in limbs.
減少四肢的疼痛和腫脹。

### Medications 藥物

| Medication 藥物 | Purpose 用途 |
|----------------|-------------|
| **Anticoagulants (blood thinners)** 抗凝血劑 | Prevent blood clots 預防血栓 |
| **Sirolimus** 西羅莫司 | Decrease pain and improve coagulation levels 減少疼痛並改善凝血水平 |

### Surgery 手術
- Resection (removal) may be used for small lesions
  切除可用於小型病變
- Specific sub-types like GVM may benefit from surgery
  特定亞型如球靜脈畸形可能受益於手術
- May be combined with sclerotherapy
  可與硬化療法結合

---

## Long-term Outlook 長期預後

- VM is a chronic condition 靜脈畸形是慢性疾病
- May require multiple treatments throughout a child's life
  可能需要在孩子一生中進行多次治療
- Lesions can expand or recur 病變可能擴大或復發
- Regular follow-up with the vascular anomalies team is important
  定期與血管畸形團隊跟進很重要

---

## When to Seek Help 何時尋求幫助

Contact your doctor if you notice:
如果您注意到以下情況，請聯繫醫生：

- Sudden increase in pain or swelling 疼痛或腫脹突然增加
- Signs of infection (redness, warmth, fever) 感染跡象（發紅、發熱、發燒）
- Difficulty with normal activities 進行正常活動有困難
- Bleeding that doesn't stop 無法止住的出血
