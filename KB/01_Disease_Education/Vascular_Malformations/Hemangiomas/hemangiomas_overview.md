# Hemangiomas (Infantile Hemangiomas)
# 血管瘤（嬰兒血管瘤）

*Source: KidsHealth (Nemours) - Reviewed by Amy W. Anzilotti, MD (November 2022)*

---

## What Is an Infantile Hemangioma? 什麼是嬰兒血管瘤？

An infantile hemangioma is a type of birthmark that occurs when a tangled group of blood vessels grows in or under a baby's skin. It is usually noticed within the first few days to months of life. While they grow larger at first, they eventually shrink and go away over time. Although they are called "tumors" because they grow and change, they are non-cancerous and do not spread.

嬰兒血管瘤是一種胎記，當一團糾結的血管在嬰兒皮膚內或皮下生長時就會形成。通常在出生後的最初幾天到幾個月內被注意到。雖然起初會變大，但最終會縮小並隨時間消退。儘管因為會生長和變化而被稱為「腫瘤」，但它們是非癌性的，不會擴散。

---

## Types of Hemangiomas 血管瘤類型

### Superficial (Cutaneous) 淺層（皮膚）
These grow on the skin's surface and are often called "strawberry hemangiomas" due to their red, bumpy appearance.
這些生長在皮膚表面，由於紅色凸起的外觀，通常被稱為「草莓血管瘤」。

### Deep (Subcutaneous) 深層（皮下）
These grow under the skin, appearing as a bulge with a blue or purple tint.
這些生長在皮膚下，呈現藍色或紫色的隆起。

### Mixed 混合型
A combination of both superficial and deep types.
淺層和深層類型的結合。

### Internal 內部
Hemangiomas can also develop in organs such as the kidneys, lungs, liver, or brain.
血管瘤也可以在腎臟、肺部、肝臟或腦部等器官中發展。

### Congenital vs. Infantile 先天性與嬰兒型
Congenital hemangiomas are present at birth, whereas infantile hemangiomas appear shortly after birth and are far more common.
先天性血管瘤在出生時就存在，而嬰兒血管瘤在出生後不久出現，更為常見。

---

## Signs & Symptoms 徵兆與症狀

- Initially, it may look like a flat blue or red patch
  最初可能看起來像扁平的藍色或紅色斑塊
- It grows rapidly during the first year, typically fastest in the first 6 months
  在第一年快速增長，通常在前6個月最快
- It then begins a slow process of "involution" (shrinking), which can continue until the child is 7 to 10 years old
  然後開始緩慢的「消退」過程，可能持續到孩子7至10歲

---

## Causes & Risk Factors 成因及風險因素

The exact cause is unknown, though it may relate to oxygen level changes during fetal development. They are more common in:
確切原因不明，但可能與胎兒發育期間的氧氣水平變化有關。以下情況更常見：

- Premature babies 早產嬰兒
- Low birth weight babies 低出生體重嬰兒
- Multiple births (twins, triplets) 多胎生產（雙胞胎、三胞胎）
- Females (more common than in males) 女性（比男性更常見）

## Diagnosis 診斷

### Visual Exam 目視檢查
Most are diagnosed by appearance and clinical history.
大多數通過外觀和臨床病史診斷。

### Imaging 影像檢查
For deep or internal hemangiomas, doctors may use Ultrasound or MRI to determine the extent of the growth.
對於深層或內部血管瘤，醫生可能使用超聲波或磁力共振成像來確定生長範圍。

---

## Treatment Options 治療選擇

Most hemangiomas do not require treatment because they go away on their own. Treatment is recommended if the hemangioma:
大多數血管瘤不需要治療，因為它們會自行消退。如果血管瘤有以下情況，建議治療：

- Blocks vision or eye movement 阻擋視力或眼球活動
- Reduces airflow through the nose or mouth 減少通過鼻子或嘴巴的氣流
- Bleeds frequently or ulcerates (breaks through the skin) 經常出血或潰瘍（穿透皮膚）
- Puts a strain on the heart (rare) 對心臟造成負擔（罕見）
- Significantly affects appearance (especially on the face) 明顯影響外觀（特別是臉部）

### Common Treatments 常見治療方法

#### Medication 藥物治療
Oral Propranolol is the most common treatment. Topical versions or steroid injections may also be used.
口服普萘洛爾是最常見的治療方法。也可使用外用版本或類固醇注射。

#### Interventional Radiology (Embolization) 介入放射學（栓塞術）
A procedure where a catheter is used to block the blood vessels supplying the hemangioma.
使用導管阻斷供應血管瘤的血管的程序。

#### Surgery 手術
To remove the hemangioma or to correct loose skin/scarring after it has shrunk.
切除血管瘤或在其縮小後矯正鬆弛的皮膚/疤痕。

#### Laser Therapy 激光治療
Often used for skin color changes or to treat ulcerated hemangiomas.
通常用於皮膚顏色變化或治療潰瘍性血管瘤。

---

## When to Seek Medical Help 何時尋求醫療幫助

Parents should consult a specialist (such as an Interventional Radiologist or Dermatologist) if the birthmark:
如果胎記有以下情況，家長應諮詢專家（如介入放射科醫生或皮膚科醫生）：

- Begins to bleed or develops a wound (ulcer) 開始出血或產生傷口（潰瘍）
- Is located near the eye, nose, or mouth 位於眼睛、鼻子或嘴巴附近
- Grows very rapidly 快速增長

---

## Outlook 預後

The prognosis is generally excellent. Most hemangiomas disappear by age 10, though they may leave behind slightly stretched skin or a faint mark that can be addressed later with cosmetic procedures if necessary.

預後一般良好。大多數血管瘤在10歲前消失，但可能留下輕微拉伸的皮膚或淡淡的痕跡，如有需要可在日後通過美容程序處理。
