# Lymphatic Malformations
# 淋巴管畸形

*Source: KidsHealth (Nemours)*

---

## What Are Lymphatic Malformations? 什麼是淋巴管畸形？

A lymphatic malformation is a benign (non-cancerous) clump of abnormal lymph vessels forming a disorganized, spongy cluster of cysts. They appear as unusual growths or masses.

淋巴管畸形是一種良性（非癌性）的異常淋巴管團塊，形成雜亂、海綿狀的囊腫群。它們表現為異常的生長或腫塊。

---

## What Happens in Lymphatic Malformations? 淋巴管畸形會發生什麼？

- The lymphatic system normally collects lymph fluid and returns it to the bloodstream
  淋巴系統通常收集淋巴液並將其返回血液

- In malformations, lymph can't drain properly because the vessels are separate from the normal system, leading to swelling (flares)
  在畸形中，由於血管與正常系統分離，淋巴液無法正常排出，導致腫脹（發作）

- They often contain fragile, malformed veins that can bleed easily, causing the malformation to fill with blood and grow
  它們通常含有脆弱、畸形的靜脈，容易出血，導致畸形充滿血液並增大

- As they grow, they can press on nearby parts like the windpipe, eyes, or blood vessels
  隨著它們的增長，可能會壓迫附近的氣管、眼睛或血管

- They can occur anywhere except the brain, but are most common in the head and neck
  它們可以出現在除大腦外的任何地方，但最常見於頭頸部

## Types of Lymphatic Malformations 淋巴管畸形類型

### Microcystic 微囊型
Spongy with small vessels and tiny cysts.
海綿狀，有小血管和微小囊腫。

### Macrocystic (Cystic Hygromas) 大囊型（囊性水瘤）
Large, stretched vessels and cysts filled with lymph, blood, or both.
大的、拉伸的血管和囊腫，充滿淋巴液、血液或兩者。

## Signs & Symptoms 徵兆與症狀

### General Symptoms 一般症狀
- Growing, spongy lump 逐漸增大的海綿狀腫塊
- Often has small surface bubbles (vesicles) which can leak clear or bloody fluid
  通常有小的表面水泡，可能滲出透明或血性液體
- Overgrowth and swelling in affected limbs, lips, or tongue
  受影響的四肢、嘴唇或舌頭過度生長和腫脹

### Location-Specific Symptoms 特定位置的症狀

| Location 位置 | Symptoms 症狀 |
|--------------|--------------|
| **Mouth/Throat 口腔/咽喉** | Difficulty breathing, swallowing, or speaking 呼吸、吞嚥或說話困難 |
| **Eye Socket 眼眶** | Double vision, bulging eye 複視、眼球突出 |
| **Chest 胸部** | Wheezing, chest pain, breathing trouble 喘息、胸痛、呼吸困難 |
| **GI/Pelvis 腸胃/骨盆** | Rectal bleeding, constipation, bladder issues 直腸出血、便秘、膀胱問題 |
| **Bones 骨骼** | Bone loss or overgrowth, rare fractures 骨質流失或過度生長、罕見骨折 |

## Causes 成因

- They form early in pregnancy; usually random and not caused by any specific maternal activity
  在懷孕早期形成；通常是隨機的，不是由任何特定的母體活動引起

- Often associated with genetic mutations (though not typically inherited)
  通常與基因突變有關（但通常不是遺傳的）

- Certain syndromes are associated, including:
  某些綜合症與之相關，包括：
  - Down syndrome 唐氏綜合症
  - Turner syndrome 特納綜合症
  - Noonan syndrome 努南綜合症

## Diagnosis 診斷

- May be seen on prenatal ultrasound or at birth
  可在產前超聲波或出生時發現

- Most are identified by age 2
  大多數在2歲前被確認

### Imaging Tests 影像檢查
- Ultrasound 超聲波
- X-rays X光
- CT scans 電腦斷層掃描
- MRI scans 磁力共振成像

---

## Treatment Options 治療選擇

### Sclerotherapy 硬化療法
Injecting medicine to cause vessels to collapse and scar (common IR procedure).
注射藥物使血管塌陷和結疤（常見的介入放射科程序）。

### Surgical Removal 手術切除
Used when organ function or appearance is significantly affected.
當器官功能或外觀受到顯著影響時使用。

### Laser Therapy / Radiofrequency Ablation 激光治療/射頻消融
Often used for skin or tongue malformations.
通常用於皮膚或舌頭畸形。

### Medicines 藥物治療
Oral medications can help shrink large malformations over time.
口服藥物可以幫助隨時間縮小大型畸形。

### Symptom Management 症狀管理
Draining fluid can temporarily reduce size for pain relief or diagnosis.
抽取液體可以暫時減小尺寸以緩解疼痛或進行診斷。

---

## Outlook 預後

- Malformations can grow back, sometimes requiring multiple treatments
  畸形可能會復發，有時需要多次治療

- Emotional support and support groups are recommended for children and families
  建議為兒童和家庭提供情感支持和支援小組

- Organizations like the Vascular Birthmarks Foundation can provide resources
  血管胎記基金會等組織可以提供資源
