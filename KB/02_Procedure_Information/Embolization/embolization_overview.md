# Catheter Embolization
# 導管栓塞術

*Source: RadiologyInfo.org (Radiological Society of North America)*

---

## What is Catheter Embolization? 什麼是導管栓塞術？

Embolization is a minimally invasive treatment that blocks one or more blood vessels or abnormal vascular channels. In a catheter embolization procedure, medications or synthetic materials called **embolic agents** are placed through a catheter into a blood vessel to prevent blood flow to the area.

栓塞術是一種微創治療，可阻斷一條或多條血管或異常血管通道。在導管栓塞程序中，稱為**栓塞劑**的藥物或合成材料通過導管放入血管中，以阻止血液流向該區域。

---

## Common Uses 常見用途

Catheter embolization can be applied to almost any part of the body to control or prevent abnormal bleeding.
導管栓塞術可應用於身體幾乎任何部位，以控制或預防異常出血。

| Condition 疾病 | Application 應用 |
|---------------|-----------------|
| **Traumatic bleeding 創傷出血** | Controlling bleeding into the abdomen or pelvis from injuries 控制因傷害導致的腹部或盆腔出血 |
| **Gastrointestinal bleeding 胃腸出血** | Treating lesions like ulcers or diverticular disease 治療潰瘍或憩室疾病等病變 |
| **Vascular malformations 血管畸形** | Eliminating abnormal connections between arteries and veins (e.g., in the lungs or brain) 消除動脈和靜脈之間的異常連接（如肺部或腦部） |
| **Tumor management 腫瘤管理** | Reducing pain from tumor bleeding or shrinking tumors by cutting off blood supply 通過切斷血液供應減少腫瘤出血的疼痛或縮小腫瘤 |
| **Aneurysms 動脈瘤** | Blocking an artery supplying an aneurysm 阻斷供應動脈瘤的動脈 |
| **Congenital venous malformations 先天性靜脈畸形** | Decreasing the size of tangled veins to reduce pain and swelling 減小纏繞靜脈的大小以減少疼痛和腫脹 |

## How to Prepare 如何準備

### Tests 檢查
Blood tests may be performed to check kidney function and clotting.
可能進行血液檢查以檢查腎功能和凝血功能。

### Medications 藥物
- Inform your doctor about all medications and supplements
  告知醫生所有藥物和補充劑
- You may need to stop taking aspirin, NSAIDs, or blood thinners
  您可能需要停止服用阿司匹林、NSAID或血液稀釋劑

### Fasting 禁食
You will likely be told not to eat or drink anything after midnight before the procedure.
您可能會被告知在程序前午夜後不要進食或飲水。

### Pregnancy 懷孕
Women must inform their doctor if there is any possibility of pregnancy to avoid fetal radiation exposure.
女性必須告知醫生是否有任何懷孕的可能，以避免胎兒輻射暴露。

### Planning 規劃
Plan for an overnight hospital stay.
計劃住院過夜。

## Equipment Used 使用的設備

| Equipment 設備 | Description 描述 |
|---------------|-----------------|
| **X-ray and Fluoroscopy X光和透視** | To guide the catheter in real-time 實時引導導管 |
| **Catheter 導管** | A long, thin plastic tube (about 3mm in diameter) 長而細的塑料管（直徑約3毫米） |
| **Embolic Agents 栓塞劑** | Gelfoam™ (temporary), particulate agents (permanent), metallic coils, liquid sclerosants, or liquid glue 明膠海綿（暫時）、顆粒劑（永久）、金屬線圈、液態硬化劑或液態膠 |

## How the Procedure Works 程序如何進行

An interventional radiologist uses X-ray imaging to guide a catheter through the skin into a blood vessel. Once at the treatment site, an embolic agent is injected to block the vessel or malformation permanently or temporarily.

介入放射科醫生使用X光成像引導導管穿過皮膚進入血管。一旦到達治療部位，注入栓塞劑以永久或暫時阻斷血管或畸形。

### Step-by-Step Procedure 程序步驟

1. **Preparation 準備**
   - You will lie on a procedure table
     您將躺在程序台上
   - Monitors will track your vitals
     監視器將追蹤您的生命體徵

2. **Sedation 鎮靜**
   - An IV line provides sedation (moderate or general anesthesia)
     IV管線提供鎮靜（中度或全身麻醉）

3. **Incision 切口**
   - A small nick is made in the skin to insert the catheter
     在皮膚上做一個小切口以插入導管

4. **Guidance 引導**
   - Contrast material is injected to locate the exact site of the abnormality via X-ray
     注入造影劑通過X光定位異常的確切位置

5. **Embolization 栓塞**
   - The embolic agent is injected through the catheter
     栓塞劑通過導管注入
   - Success is confirmed with follow-up X-rays
     通過後續X光確認成功

6. **Closure 閉合**
   - The catheter is removed
     移除導管
   - Pressure or a closure device is applied to the site
     在部位施加壓力或使用閉合裝置
   - No stitches are required
     不需要縫線

## What to Expect 預期情況

### During the Procedure 程序期間
- Slight pinch during IV insertion IV插入時輕微刺痛
- Feeling of warmth as contrast material passes through your body
  造影劑通過身體時的溫暖感覺

### After the Procedure 程序後
- Pain is common but usually controlled with medication
  疼痛很常見，但通常可以用藥物控制
- **Post-embolization syndrome** 栓塞後綜合症
  - Fever, nausea, vomiting may occur in about 20% of patients
    約20%的患者可能出現發燒、噁心、嘔吐
  - Typically clears within three days 通常在三天內消退

### Recovery 恢復
- Most patients leave within 24 hours 大多數患者在24小時內出院
- Can resume normal activities within a week 一週內可恢復正常活動

## Benefits 益處

| Benefit 益處 | Details 詳情 |
|-------------|-------------|
| **Highly effective 高效** | Excellent at controlling emergency bleeding 非常有效地控制緊急出血 |
| **Less invasive 微創** | Much less invasive than open surgery with fewer complications 比開放手術侵入性小得多，併發症更少 |
| **Brief hospital stay 短暫住院** | No large surgical incisions 無大型手術切口 |
| **Treats difficult areas 治療困難區域** | Can treat tumors or malformations that are surgically unreachable 可以治療手術無法到達的腫瘤或畸形 |

## Risks 風險

| Risk 風險 | Description 描述 |
|----------|-----------------|
| **Allergic reaction 過敏反應** | Slight risk to contrast material 對造影劑的輕微風險 |
| **Catheter risks 導管風險** | Damage to blood vessel, bruising, bleeding, or infection 血管損傷、淤青、出血或感染 |
| **Non-target embolization 非目標栓塞** | Risk that embolic agent lodges in the wrong place, depriving normal tissue of oxygen 栓塞劑進入錯誤位置的風險，剝奪正常組織的氧氣 |

---

## Limitations 限制

- Success depends on precise catheter placement
  成功取決於精確的導管放置

- In a small percentage of cases, the procedure may not be possible if the catheter cannot reach the target area
  在少數情況下，如果導管無法到達目標區域，可能無法進行程序

- Multiple sessions may be required for complex AVMs
  複雜的AVM可能需要多次治療
