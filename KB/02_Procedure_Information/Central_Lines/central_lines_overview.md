# Central Lines (Central Venous Catheters)
# 中央靜脈導管

*Source: KidsHealth (Nemours Children's Health)*

---

## What Are Central Lines? 什麼是中央靜脈導管？

A central line (or central venous catheter/CVC) is like an intravenous (IV) line. But it is much longer than a regular IV and goes all the way up to a vein near the heart or just inside the heart.

中央靜脈導管（或CVC）類似於靜脈注射（IV）管線。但它比普通IV長得多，一直延伸到靠近心臟或心臟內部的靜脈。

A patient can get medicine, fluids, blood, or nutrition through a central line. It also can be used to draw blood.

患者可以通過中央靜脈導管獲得藥物、液體、血液或營養。它也可以用於抽血。

---

## Types of Central Lines 中央靜脈導管類型

### Implanted Ports 植入式端口
A device placed entirely under the skin with access through a special needle. Ideal for long-term use.
完全放置在皮膚下的裝置，通過特殊針頭進入。適合長期使用。

### Tunneled Central Lines (External Central Lines) 隧道式中央導管（外部中央導管）
A tube that goes under the skin for a short distance before entering a large vein. Part of the tube remains outside the body.
管子在皮膚下行走一小段距離後進入大靜脈。部分管子留在體外。

### PICC Lines (Peripherally Inserted Central Catheter) PICC管（經外周插入的中央導管）
Inserted through a vein in the arm and threaded up to a large vein near the heart.
通過手臂靜脈插入，穿線至靠近心臟的大靜脈。

## When Are Central Lines Used? 何時使用中央靜脈導管？

Doctors might use a central line instead of a regular IV line because:
醫生可能使用中央靜脈導管而不是普通IV管線，因為：

- **Longer duration:** Can stay in place up to a year or even more
  **更長時間：** 可以保持一年甚至更長時間

- **Blood draws:** Makes it easier to draw blood
  **抽血：** 更容易抽血

- **Special medications:** Patients can get large amounts of fluids or medicines (like chemotherapy) that might not go through regular IVs
  **特殊藥物：** 患者可以獲得大量液體或藥物（如化療），這些可能無法通過普通IV

### Common Reasons for Central Lines 需要中央靜脈導管的常見原因

| Reason 原因 | Example 例子 |
|------------|-------------|
| **Serious infection 嚴重感染** | IV antibiotics for several weeks IV抗生素數週 |
| **Cancer 癌症** | Chemotherapy and blood tests 化療和血液檢查 |
| **Nutrition 營養** | IV nutrition (TPN) IV營養（TPN） |
| **Blood transfusions 輸血** | Multiple transfusions needed 需要多次輸血 |

## Risks and Safety 風險和安全

Most of the time, central lines do not cause any problems. If problems do happen, it is usually because:
大多數情況下，中央靜脈導管不會引起任何問題。如果出現問題，通常是因為：

- The line gets **infected** 管線受到**感染**
- The line **stops working** 管線**停止工作**
- Very rarely: a **blood clot** forms 非常罕見：形成**血栓**

> Doctors review all risks with families before placing the central line.
> 醫生在放置中央靜脈導管之前會與家屬討論所有風險。

## How Parents Can Help 家長如何幫助

If your child has a central line, you can help care for it to prevent infection and keep it working well.

如果您的孩子有中央靜脈導管，您可以幫助護理它以預防感染並保持良好運作。

### Key Care Instructions 主要護理指導

#### Ask Your Care Team 詢問護理團隊
- How often to change the dressing 多久更換敷料
- When and how to flush the line 何時以及如何沖洗管線
- What to do if the line gets blocked or comes out 如果管線堵塞或脫落該怎麼辦

#### Medication at Home 家庭用藥
Learn how to give medicines through the line if required at home.
如果需要在家中通過管線給藥，請學習如何操作。

#### Activity Restrictions 活動限制
- Most children need to avoid rough play and contact sports
  大多數孩子需要避免劇烈運動和接觸性運動
- Confirm with your team which activities are safe
  與您的團隊確認哪些活動是安全的

#### Bathing 沐浴
- Ask about special care or covers needed to protect the line
  詢問保護管線所需的特殊護理或覆蓋物
- Keep the line and dressing dry
  保持管線和敷料乾燥

#### Infection Signs 感染跡象
Watch for and report immediately:
注意並立即報告：
- [ ] Redness at the site 部位發紅
- [ ] Swelling 腫脹
- [ ] Fever 發燒
- [ ] Pain 疼痛
- [ ] Drainage 滲出物

#### School 學校
- Inform teachers and school nurses about the central line
  告知老師和學校護士關於中央靜脈導管
- They can help protect the line and support the child
  他們可以幫助保護管線並支持孩子

## Supplies & Support 用品和支援

You'll get supplies to use at home, and a visiting nurse may come to help you when you first get home.
您將獲得在家使用的用品，當您剛回家時，可能會有護士來幫助您。
