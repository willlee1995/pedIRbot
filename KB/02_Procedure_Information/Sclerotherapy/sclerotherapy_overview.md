# Sclerotherapy for Vascular Malformations
# 血管畸形硬化療法

*Source: Boston Children's Hospital*

---

## What is Sclerotherapy? 什麼是硬化療法？

Sclerotherapy is a minimally invasive procedure used to treat vascular malformations in children. During the procedure, an interventional radiologist injects a medication called a **sclerosing agent** (or sclerosant) directly into the malformation, causing the abnormal vessel or channel to shrink and gradually close over time.

硬化療法是一種用於治療兒童血管畸形的微創程序。在程序中，介入放射科醫生將稱為**硬化劑**的藥物直接注入畸形中，導致異常血管或通道收縮並逐漸閉合。

---

## What Conditions Does Sclerotherapy Treat? 硬化療法治療什麼疾病？

It is most commonly used for certain types of vascular malformations, particularly:
它最常用於治療某些類型的血管畸形，特別是：

- **Venous malformations** 靜脈畸形
- **Lymphatic malformations** 淋巴管畸形

---

## How Does Sclerotherapy Work? 硬化療法如何運作？

1. The sclerosing agent causes the malformation to swell briefly
   硬化劑導致畸形短暫腫脹

2. Then it slowly shrinks and closes
   然後它慢慢縮小並閉合

3. Over time, the child's body safely absorbs the sclerosant
   隨著時間推移，孩子的身體安全地吸收硬化劑

### Results 效果
- Reduces swelling 減少腫脹
- Reduces discomfort 減少不適
- Reduces visible or felt fullness 減少可見或感覺到的飽滿

> **Note:** Some children may require more than one treatment session depending on the size, type, and location of the malformation.
> **注意：** 根據畸形的大小、類型和位置，一些孩子可能需要多次治療。

## Before the Procedure (Preparation) 程序前（準備）

### Imaging 影像檢查
- Ultrasound or MRI may be ordered to plan the treatment
  可能會安排超聲波或MRI來規劃治療

### Pre-operative Evaluation 術前評估
- Lab tests may be needed for anesthesia safety
  可能需要進行實驗室檢查以確保麻醉安全

### Instructions 指導
- A care team member will call a few days prior to review:
  護理團隊成員將在數天前致電審查：
  - Fasting requirements 禁食要求
  - Medications 藥物
  - Anesthesia explanation 麻醉說明

### Illness 疾病
> If the child becomes ill within three days of the procedure, inform the provider as rescheduling may be necessary.
> 如果孩子在程序前三天內生病，請通知醫療提供者，可能需要重新安排。

---

## On the Day of the Procedure 程序當天

### Before the Procedure 程序前
1. Families meet the anesthesia team and the interventional radiologist
   家屬與麻醉團隊和介入放射科醫生會面

2. Review the sedation plan and sign consent forms
   審查鎮靜計劃並簽署同意書

3. The child will change into a hospital gown
   孩子將換上醫院病號服

4. An IV line will be placed and anesthesia given
   將放置IV管線並給予麻醉

### During the Procedure 程序期間
- The interventional radiologist uses **ultrasound and X-ray imaging** to guide a needle or catheter into the treatment area
  介入放射科醫生使用**超聲波和X光影像**引導針或導管進入治療區域

- **Contrast solution** may be used for clarity
  可能使用**造影劑**以便清晰顯示

- Once the sclerosant is injected, the needle or catheter is removed
  一旦注入硬化劑，就會移除針或導管

- A small bandage is applied
  貼上小繃帶

### Duration 時間
Typically takes **1 to 4 hours**.
通常需要**1至4小時**。

---

## After the Procedure (Recovery) 程序後（恢復）

### Recovery Room 恢復室
- The child stays in the recovery room until they are ready to go home
  孩子留在恢復室直到準備好回家

- The radiologist will discuss the procedure's outcome with the parents
  放射科醫生將與家長討論程序的結果

### Expected Side Effects 預期的副作用
These are common and usually last for **1 to 3 weeks**:
這些很常見，通常持續**1至3週**：

| Side Effect 副作用 | Description 描述 |
|-------------------|------------------|
| **Swelling 腫脹** | Normal response to treatment 對治療的正常反應 |
| **Tenderness 壓痛** | The area may be sore 該區域可能會疼痛 |
| **Bruising 淤青** | Common and temporary 常見且暫時 |
| **Firmness 硬塊感** | The area may feel firm or "full" as it shrinks 該區域在縮小時可能感覺堅硬或「飽滿」 |

### Comfort 舒適
- Acetaminophen (paracetamol) may be used for discomfort as prescribed
  可按處方使用撲熱息痛緩解不適

## Risks and Safety 風險和安全

Sclerotherapy is generally safe but carries risks specific to:
硬化療法通常是安全的，但有以下特定風險：

- The sclerosing agent used 使用的硬化劑
- Anesthesia 麻醉

> Imaging uses the lowest possible dose of X-ray radiation to ensure safety for children.
> 影像檢查使用最低可能劑量的X光輻射以確保兒童安全。

## Frequently Asked Questions 常見問題

### Will my child feel pain? 我的孩子會感到疼痛嗎？
Children are under anesthesia and comfortable during the procedure.
孩子在程序期間處於麻醉狀態，感覺舒適。

### How long does it take? 需要多長時間？
Typically 1 to 4 hours.
通常1至4小時。

### Will my child need multiple sessions? 我的孩子需要多次治療嗎？
Multiple sessions may be needed for larger or complex malformations.
較大或複雜的畸形可能需要多次治療。

### When can my child return to normal activities? 我的孩子何時可以恢復正常活動？
The care team provides specific guidance on returning to school and physical activities.
護理團隊會提供關於返回學校和體育活動的具體指導。
