# Interventional Radiology Procedure Preparation
# 介入放射科程序準備

*Source: RadiologyInfo.org (Radiological Society of North America)*

---

## Overview 概述

Interventional radiology (IR) procedures use imaging guidance (such as X-ray, ultrasound, or CT) to perform minimally invasive treatments. Common IR procedures include catheter angiography, angioplasty, vascular stenting, and embolization.

介入放射科（IR）程序使用成像引導（如X光、超聲波或CT）進行微創治療。常見的IR程序包括導管血管造影、血管成形術、血管支架植入和栓塞術。

---

## Before the Procedure (Preparation) 程序前（準備）

### Fasting 禁食
- Patients are typically instructed not to eat or drink anything for **4 to 8 hours** before the procedure
  患者通常被指示在程序前**4至8小時**不要進食或飲水
- This is especially important if sedation is planned
  如果計劃使用鎮靜劑，這一點尤其重要

### Medications 藥物
- Discuss all medications (including blood thinners/NSAIDs) with your doctor
  與醫生討論所有藥物（包括血液稀釋劑/NSAID）
- Most regular medications (like blood pressure meds) can be taken with small sips of water
  大多數常規藥物（如血壓藥）可以用少量水服用
- Blood thinners may need to be stopped several days before
  血液稀釋劑可能需要在數天前停用

### Allergy & Health Screening 過敏和健康篩查
- **Critical:** Disclose any allergies to iodine or contrast materials
  **重要：** 披露對碘或造影劑的任何過敏
- Inform about any recent illnesses or kidney disease
  告知最近的疾病或腎病
- Bring a list of all current medications
  帶上所有目前藥物的清單

### Pregnancy 懷孕
- Women must inform their doctor if there is any possibility of pregnancy
  女性必須告知醫生是否有懷孕的可能
- This is to minimize radiation risk to the fetus
  這是為了最小化對胎兒的輻射風險

### Practical Considerations 實際考慮
- Wear comfortable, loose clothing 穿寬鬆舒適的衣服
- Leave jewelry at home 將珠寶留在家中
- A driver is required for the trip home if sedation is used
  如果使用鎮靜劑，需要有人開車送您回家

## During the Procedure 程序期間

### Access 進入點
- A small incision (a few millimeters) is made, usually in the **groin or arm**
  在**腹股溝或手臂**做一個小切口（幾毫米）
- The area is numbed with local anesthetic
  該區域用局部麻醉劑麻醉

### Guidance 引導
- A thin, flexible tube called a **catheter** is guided through blood vessels
  一根細而柔韌的管子稱為**導管**通過血管引導
- Live X-ray imaging (fluoroscopy) is used to see the catheter
  使用實時X光成像（透視）來觀察導管

### What Happens 發生什麼
- **Angiography 血管造影:** Contrast material is injected through the catheter to highlight blood vessels on X-rays
  造影劑通過導管注入以在X光上突出血管
- **Angioplasty/Stenting 血管成形術/支架:** A balloon-tipped catheter opens blocked vessels. A metal mesh tube (stent) may be permanently placed
  帶球囊的導管打開阻塞的血管。可能永久放置金屬網管（支架）
- **Embolization 栓塞術:** Materials are used to block abnormal blood vessels
  使用材料阻斷異常血管

### What You May Feel 您可能感覺到什麼
- Stinging sensation from the local anesthetic 局部麻醉劑的刺痛感
- Brief "warmth" or "burning" feeling when contrast is injected 注射造影劑時短暫的「溫暖」或「燒灼」感
- The most challenging part is often having to lie flat for the procedure
  最具挑戰性的部分通常是必須在程序期間平躺

## After the Procedure (Recovery) 程序後（恢復）

### Immediate Care 即時護理
- Pressure is applied to the incision site for 10–20 minutes to prevent bleeding
  在切口處施加壓力10-20分鐘以防止出血
- Patients must remain in bed (often lying flat) for several hours for monitoring
  患者必須臥床（通常平躺）數小時以進行監測

### Diet & Fluids 飲食與液體
- You can normally resume your diet immediately 通常可以立即恢復飲食
- Drink plenty of fluids to help flush the contrast material from your kidneys
  多喝液體以幫助將造影劑從腎臟沖出

### Activity Level 活動水平
- Avoid strenuous activity or heavy lifting for at least **24 hours**
  至少**24小時**內避免劇烈活動或重物搬運
- Normal activities can typically be resumed after 8–12 hours
  通常可以在8-12小時後恢復正常活動

### What to Expect 預期情況
- Bruising at the site is common and temporary 部位淤青很常見且是暫時的
- Watch for signs of bleeding, swelling, or changes in limb color
  注意出血、腫脹或肢體顏色變化的跡象

## Risks and Benefits 風險與益處

### Benefits 益處
| Benefit | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Minimally invasive 微創** | Alternative to open surgery 開放手術的替代方案 |
| **Accurate imaging 準確影像** | Extremely detailed views of blood vessels 血管的非常詳細視圖 |
| **Combined diagnosis and treatment 結合診斷和治療** | Done in a single procedure 在單一程序中完成 |
| **Faster recovery 更快恢復** | Less recovery time than traditional surgery 比傳統手術恢復時間短 |

### Risks 風險
| Risk | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Allergic reactions 過敏反應** | Rare reaction to iodinated contrast material 對碘化造影劑的罕見反應 |
| **Kidney impact 腎臟影響** | Contrast can cause temporary kidney injury, particularly in patients with kidney disease or diabetes 造影劑可能導致暫時性腎損傷，特別是在腎病或糖尿病患者中 |
| **Procedural risks 程序風險** | Small risk of blood clots, vessel damage, or bleeding at puncture site 血栓、血管損傷或穿刺部位出血的小風險 |
| **Radiation 輻射** | Minimal exposure to ionizing radiation 最小的電離輻射暴露 |

## When to Call the Doctor 何時聯繫醫生

Contact your healthcare provider immediately if you experience:
如果您出現以下情況，請立即聯繫醫療保健提供者：

- [ ] Bleeding at the puncture site that doesn't stop with pressure
      壓迫後穿刺部位仍出血不止
- [ ] Severe pain at the access site 進入點嚴重疼痛
- [ ] Changes in color, temperature, or feeling in the affected limb
      受影響肢體的顏色、溫度或感覺變化
- [ ] Fever or signs of infection 發燒或感染跡象
- [ ] Difficulty breathing 呼吸困難
- [ ] Chest pain 胸痛
