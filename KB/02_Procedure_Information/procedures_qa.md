## Q1: What is interventional radiology?
## 問題1：什麼是介入放射治療？

**Answer 答案:**

Interventional radiology (IR) is a medical specialty that uses imaging guidance (X-ray, ultrasound, CT, MRI) to perform minimally invasive procedures. Instead of traditional open surgery, IR doctors insert small needles or catheters through tiny incisions to diagnose and treat conditions.

**Benefits 優點:**
- Smaller incisions (often just a needle puncture) 較小切口（通常只是針刺）
- Less pain 較少疼痛
- Faster recovery 更快康復
- Often done as outpatient (go home same day) 通常作為門診進行（當天回家）
- Lower risk of complications 併發症風險較低

介入放射治療是一門醫學專科，使用影像引導（X光、超聲波、CT、MRI）進行微創手術。介入放射治療醫生通過微小切口插入小針或導管來診斷和治療疾病，而非傳統開放手術。

## Q2: What is a central line and why might my child need one?
## 問題2：什麼是中央靜脈導管？為什麼我的孩子可能需要？

**Answer 答案:**

A central line (also called central venous catheter) is a tube placed in a large vein, usually in the chest, neck, or upper arm. It provides reliable access to give medications and draw blood.

**Types 類型:**
| Type 類型 | Description 描述 |
|-----------|------------------|
| **PICC line** | Inserted through arm vein 通過手臂靜脈插入 |
| **Tunneled catheter** | Inserted in chest, tunneled under skin 在胸部插入，在皮下隧道 |
| **Port (Port-a-cath)** | Completely under skin, accessed with needle 完全在皮下，用針進入 |

**Used for 用於:**
- Long-term IV medications 長期靜脈注射藥物
- Chemotherapy 化療
- Frequent blood draws 頻繁抽血
- IV nutrition 靜脈營養

中央靜脈導管是放置在大靜脈中的管子，通常在胸部、頸部或上臂。它提供可靠的通道來給藥和抽血。

## Q3: What is sclerotherapy used for in children?
## 問題3：硬化療法在兒童中有什麼用途？

**Answer 答案:**

Sclerotherapy is primarily used to treat:

**Lymphatic malformations 淋巴管畸形:**
- Large cystic masses (macrocystic) respond best 大囊性腫塊（大囊型）反應最佳
- Multiple sessions often needed 通常需要多次療程

**Venous malformations 靜脈畸形:**
- Abnormal vein clusters 異常靜脈簇
- Often combined with compression therapy 通常與壓迫療法結合

**How it works 如何運作:**
A sclerosing agent is injected directly into the malformation, causing the abnormal vessels to scar closed and shrink over time.

**What to expect 預期情況:**
- Done under general anesthesia for children 兒童在全身麻醉下進行
- Swelling and bruising for 1-2 weeks 腫脹和瘀青1-2周
- Usually outpatient 通常為門診

## Q4: What is embolization? What conditions is it used for?
## 問題4：什麼是栓塞術？它用於什麼疾病？

**Answer 答案:**

Embolization is a procedure where materials are delivered through a catheter to block abnormal blood vessels.

**Used for 用於:**
- **AVMs** (arteriovenous malformations) 動靜脈畸形
- **Tumors** (to shrink before surgery or control bleeding) 腫瘤（手術前縮小或控制出血）
- **Bleeding** (to stop active hemorrhage) 出血（停止活動性出血）
- **Varicoceles** 精索靜脈曲張

**Embolic materials 栓塞物質:**
- Coils 線圈
- Particles 顆粒
- Liquid agents (glue, Onyx) 液體製劑（膠水、Onyx）

**Process 過程:**
A catheter is inserted through a blood vessel (usually groin or arm) and navigated to the target area using X-ray guidance. The embolic material is then injected to block the abnormal vessels.

栓塞術是通過導管輸送物質來阻塞異常血管的手術。

## Q5: What is a biopsy and how is it done by interventional radiology?
## 問題5：什麼是活檢？介入放射治療如何進行？

**Answer 答案:**

A biopsy is taking a small sample of tissue to test for disease. Interventional radiology performs image-guided biopsies using ultrasound or CT to precisely target the area.

**How it's done 如何進行:**
1. Imaging identifies the target area 影像識別目標區域
2. Skin is numbed with local anesthetic 皮膚用局部麻醉藥麻痺
3. A thin needle is inserted through the skin 細針通過皮膚插入
4. Using real-time imaging, the needle is guided to the target 使用實時影像，將針引導到目標
5. A small tissue sample is taken 取出小組織樣本
6. The needle is removed and bandage applied 移除針並貼上繃帶

**Benefits over surgical biopsy 比手術活檢的優點:**
- No general anesthesia needed in older children 較大兒童不需全身麻醉
- Smaller incision 較小切口
- Faster recovery 更快康復
- Can target deep areas 可以針對深層區域

活檢是取出小組織樣本以檢測疾病。介入放射治療使用超聲波或CT進行影像引導活檢，精確針對目標區域。

## Q6: How long do interventional radiology procedures take?
## 問題6：介入放射治療手術需要多長時間？

**Answer 答案:**

| Procedure 手術 | Typical Duration 一般時間 |
|----------------|---------------------------|
| PICC line placement PICC導管置入 | 30-60 minutes 30-60分鐘 |
| Port placement 植入式輸液港置入 | 45-90 minutes 45-90分鐘 |
| Simple sclerotherapy 簡單硬化療法 | 30-60 minutes 30-60分鐘 |
| Complex sclerotherapy 複雜硬化療法 | 1-2 hours 1-2小時 |
| Embolization 栓塞術 | 1-3 hours 1-3小時 |
| Biopsy 活檢 | 30-60 minutes 30-60分鐘 |

Note: These times are for the procedure only. Add time for preparation, anesthesia, and recovery.
注意：這些時間僅為手術時間。需加上準備、麻醉和恢復時間。

## Q7: Will my child be awake during the procedure?
## 問題7：我的孩子在手術期間會是清醒的嗎？

**Answer 答案:**

This depends on your child's age and the type of procedure:

**General anesthesia (completely asleep) 全身麻醉（完全入睡）:**
- Young children (typically under 10-12 years) 幼童（通常10-12歲以下）
- Complex or lengthy procedures 複雜或長時間手術
- Procedures requiring complete stillness 需要完全靜止的手術

**Sedation (sleepy but may be slightly aware) 鎮靜（睏倦但可能略有意識）:**
- Some shorter procedures in older children 較大兒童的一些較短手術
- Child is relaxed and comfortable 孩子放鬆舒適

**Local anesthesia only (awake) 僅局部麻醉（清醒）:**
- Sometimes for older teenagers 有時用於較大青少年
- Simple procedures like some biopsies 簡單手術如某些活檢

Your medical team will discuss the best option for your child.

## Q8: How many treatments will my child need?
## 問題8：我的孩子需要多少次治療？

**Answer 答案:**

This varies greatly depending on the condition:

| Condition 疾病 | Typical Treatment Course 一般治療過程 |
|----------------|--------------------------------------|
| **Central line placement 中央靜脈導管置入** | Single procedure 單次手術 |
| **Biopsy 活檢** | Usually single procedure 通常單次手術 |
| **Lymphatic malformation (sclerotherapy) 淋巴管畸形（硬化療法）** | 2-4 sessions, 6-8 weeks apart 2-4次療程，間隔6-8周 |
| **Venous malformation (sclerotherapy) 靜脈畸形（硬化療法）** | Multiple sessions over months/years 數月/數年內多次療程 |
| **AVM (embolization) 動靜脈畸形（栓塞術）** | Multiple sessions, ongoing monitoring 多次療程，持續監測 |

Your HKCH team will develop an individualized treatment plan.
香港兒童醫院團隊會制定個性化治療計劃。

## Q9: What are the risks of interventional radiology procedures?
## 問題9：介入放射治療手術有什麼風險？

**Answer 答案:**

While IR procedures are generally very safe, all medical procedures have some risks:

**General risks 一般風險:**
- Bleeding at puncture site 穿刺部位出血
- Infection 感染
- Bruising 瘀青
- Allergic reaction to contrast dye 對造影劑過敏反應
- Anesthesia risks 麻醉風險

**Procedure-specific risks 特定手術風險:**
- **Sclerotherapy:** Swelling, skin discoloration, nerve irritation 腫脹、皮膚變色、神經刺激
- **Embolization:** Post-embolization syndrome (fever, fatigue) 栓塞後綜合症（發燒、疲勞）
- **Central lines:** Infection, clot formation, line malfunction 感染、血栓形成、導管故障

Your medical team will discuss specific risks for your child's procedure.
醫療團隊會討論孩子手術的具體風險。

## Q10: Can my child go home the same day after the procedure?
## 問題10：孩子手術後可以當天回家嗎？

**Answer 答案:**

Most interventional radiology procedures are done on an outpatient basis (same-day discharge). However, overnight stay may be needed for:

- Complex procedures requiring extended monitoring 需要延長監測的複雜手術
- Procedures late in the day 當天較晚進行的手術
- Children with other medical conditions 有其他醫療狀況的孩子
- If recovery doesn't go as expected 如果康復不如預期
- Some embolization procedures 部分栓塞手術

Your medical team will let you know in advance if overnight stay is expected.

大多數介入放射治療手術以門診方式進行（當天出院）。但以下情況可能需要過夜：

- 需要延長監測的複雜手術
- 當天較晚進行的手術
- 有其他醫療狀況的孩子
- 如果康復不如預期
- 部分栓塞手術

醫療團隊會提前告知是否預計需要過夜。
