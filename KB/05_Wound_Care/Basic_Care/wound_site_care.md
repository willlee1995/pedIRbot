# Wound and Puncture Site Care
# 傷口及穿刺部位護理

**Source 資料來源:** SickKids/AboutKidsHealth, CHOP, Texas Children's
**Category 類別:** Wound Care 傷口護理
**Language 語言:** Bilingual English/Traditional Chinese 中英雙語

---

## Overview 概述

Proper care of your child's procedure site is essential for healing and preventing infection. This guide covers caring for puncture sites from interventional radiology procedures such as angiography, embolization, and central line placement.

正確護理孩子的手術部位對傷口癒合和預防感染至關重要。本指南涵蓋介入放射治療程序（如血管造影、栓塞和中央靜脈導管置入）穿刺部位的護理。

---

## First 24-48 Hours 首24-48小時

### Keep the Dressing On 保持敷料完整

| Time 時間 | Instruction 指示 |
|-----------|------------------|
| **First 24 hours 首24小時** | Keep original dressing clean and dry 保持原敷料清潔乾爽 |
| **24-48 hours 24-48小時** | Dressing can usually be removed 通常可移除敷料 |

> ⚠️ **Important 重要:** Do not get the dressing wet during this time.
> 此期間不要弄濕敷料。

### If the Dressing Gets Wet or Dirty 如敷料弄濕或弄髒

1. Remove the wet dressing carefully 小心移除濕敷料
2. Gently clean the area with mild soap and water 用溫和肥皂和清水輕輕清潔該區域
3. Pat dry with a clean towel 用乾淨毛巾輕拍乾
4. Apply a clean adhesive bandage (Band-Aid) 貼上乾淨的膠布（創可貼）

---

## After Dressing Removal 移除敷料後

### Daily Cleaning 每日清潔

**Steps 步驟:**

1. ✋ Wash your hands thoroughly before and after touching the site 觸摸傷口前後徹底洗手
2. 🧼 Gently wash the site with mild soap and water 用溫和肥皂和清水輕輕清洗傷口
3. 🚿 Rinse well 徹底沖洗
4. 🧻 Pat dry gently - do not rub 輕拍乾 - 不要擦拭
5. 🩹 Cover with a clean bandage if needed 如有需要用乾淨膠布覆蓋

### Products to Avoid 避免使用的產品

| ❌ Avoid 避免 | Reason 原因 |
|---------------|-------------|
| Lotions 乳液 | Can trap bacteria 可能困住細菌 |
| Powders 爽身粉 | Can irritate the site 可能刺激傷口 |
| Antibiotic ointments (unless prescribed) 抗生素藥膏（除非處方） | Follow doctor's instructions 遵循醫生指示 |
| Hydrogen peroxide 雙氧水 | Can delay healing 可能延遲癒合 |
| Rubbing alcohol 酒精 | Too harsh for healing skin 對癒合中的皮膚過於刺激 |

## Steri-Strips (White Tape Strips) 免縫膠布（白色膠條）

If Steri-Strips were applied to your child's wound:
如孩子的傷口貼有免縫膠布：

### Care Instructions 護理指示

| Action 行動 | Instruction 指示 |
|-------------|------------------|
| **Removal 移除** | Let them fall off naturally 讓它們自然脫落 |
| **Timeline 時間** | Usually 7-10 days 通常7-10天 |
| **If still on after 10 days 如10天後仍未脫落** | Soak in bath and gently remove 浸泡在水中後輕輕移除 |
| **If edges lift early 如邊緣提早翹起** | Do not pull - trim loose edges with clean scissors 不要拉扯 - 用乾淨剪刀修剪鬆脫的邊緣 |

## Bathing and Showering 洗澡和淋浴

### Timeline 時間表

| Activity 活動 | When Allowed 何時可以 | Notes 備註 |
|---------------|----------------------|------------|
| 🚿 **Showering 淋浴** | 24-48 hours after procedure 手術後24-48小時 | Keep water brief on site 讓水短暫接觸傷口 |
| 🛁 **Bathing 泡澡** | At least 1-2 weeks 至少1-2周 | No soaking until fully healed 完全癒合前不要浸泡 |
| 🏊 **Swimming 游泳** | At least 2 weeks 至少2周 | Infection risk from pool/ocean 泳池/海水有感染風險 |
| ♨️ **Hot tubs 熱水浴缸** | At least 2 weeks 至少2周 | Bacteria can enter wound 細菌可能進入傷口 |

### Why No Soaking? 為什麼不能浸泡？

- Prolonged water exposure softens the healing tissue 長時間接觸水會軟化癒合組織
- Pools, baths, and oceans contain bacteria that can cause infection 泳池、浴缸和海水含有可能導致感染的細菌
- Wait until the site is completely healed and any scabs have fallen off 等待傷口完全癒合及結痂脫落

## Signs of Infection 感染徵兆

### Watch For These Warning Signs 注意以下警號

Contact your doctor or seek medical care if you notice:
如發現以下情況，請聯繫醫生或尋求醫療護理：

| Sign 徵兆 | Description 描述 |
|-----------|------------------|
| 🌡️ **Fever 發燒** | Temperature higher than 38°C (100.4°F) 體溫高於38°C |
| 🔴 **Redness 發紅** | Increasing redness spreading from the site 從傷口擴散的紅腫增加 |
| 🔥 **Warmth 發熱** | Site feels hot to touch 觸摸時傷口感覺發熱 |
| 🫧 **Swelling 腫脹** | New or increasing swelling 新出現或增加的腫脹 |
| 💛 **Drainage 分泌物** | Yellow or green pus, foul-smelling discharge 黃色或綠色膿液、有異味的分泌物 |
| 😰 **Pain 疼痛** | Pain getting worse instead of better 疼痛惡化而非好轉 |

> 🚨 **Seek Immediate Care 立即尋求醫療護理** if your child has:
> - High fever with shaking/chills 高燒伴有發抖/寒顫
> - Red streaks spreading from the site 從傷口擴散的紅線
> - Severe pain 劇烈疼痛

## Bleeding at the Site 傷口出血

### If Minor Bleeding Occurs 如出現輕微出血

**Steps 步驟:**

1. 🛑 Apply firm, direct pressure with a clean cloth or gauze 用乾淨的布或紗布直接用力按壓
2. ⏱️ Hold pressure for 10-15 minutes without lifting to check 持續按壓10-15分鐘，不要抬起查看
3. ✅ If bleeding stops, apply a clean bandage 如出血停止，貼上乾淨膠布

### When to Seek Emergency Care 何時尋求緊急護理

Go to the emergency department or call 999 if:
如出現以下情況，請到急症室或致電999：

- ❌ Bleeding does not stop after 15 minutes of firm pressure 用力按壓15分鐘後出血仍未停止
- ❌ Blood is spurting from the site 血液從傷口噴出
- ❌ A large lump develops at the site 傷口出現大腫塊

## Circulation Check (For Groin/Arm Procedures) 血液循環檢查（腹股溝/手臂手術）

If the procedure was done through the groin or arm, check the limb regularly:
如手術從腹股溝或手臂進行，請定期檢查肢體：

### Normal Signs ✅ 正常徵兆

- Warm to touch 觸摸時溫暖
- Normal skin color 正常膚色
- Can move fingers/toes freely 可自由活動手指/腳趾
- No numbness or tingling 沒有麻痺或刺痛

### Warning Signs ❌ 警號

Contact your doctor if the hand or foot becomes:
如手或腳出現以下情況，請聯繫醫生：

- Cold 冰冷
- Pale or bluish 蒼白或發藍
- Numb or tingly 麻痺或刺痛
- Painful with no relief from medication 疼痛且藥物無法緩解

## Scar Care After Healing 傷口癒合後的疤痕護理

### Sun Protection 防曬保護

> ☀️ **Critical 關鍵:** Protect new scars from direct sunlight for **ONE FULL YEAR**.
> 保護新疤痕免受陽光直射**整整一年**。

**Why 原因:**
- New scars burn easily 新疤痕容易曬傷
- Sun exposure can cause permanent darkening 陽光照射可導致永久變黑

**How 方法:**
- Use SPF 30+ sunscreen on the scar 在疤痕上使用SPF 30+防曬霜
- Cover with clothing when possible 盡可能用衣物遮蓋
- Apply sunscreen even on cloudy days 即使陰天也要塗防曬霜

### Scar Massage 疤痕按摩

Once the wound is completely healed and closed:
傷口完全癒合閉合後：

- Gently massage the scar in small circles 以小圓圈輕輕按摩疤痕
- Do this for a few minutes, 2-3 times daily 每次數分鐘，每日2-3次
- Use a fragrance-free moisturizer 使用無香料潤膚霜
- This helps soften the scar tissue 這有助軟化疤痕組織

### Silicone Products 矽膠產品

Some doctors recommend:
部分醫生建議：

- Silicone scar sheets 矽膠疤痕貼
- Silicone scar gel 矽膠疤痕凝膠

Discuss with your healthcare team if these would benefit your child.
與醫療團隊討論這些產品是否適合您的孩子。

## Common Questions 常見問題

| Question 問題 | Answer 答案 |
|---------------|-------------|
| **When can my child shower? 孩子何時可淋浴？** | Usually 24-48 hours after the procedure 通常手術後24-48小時 |
| **Does the site need to be covered? 傷口需要覆蓋嗎？** | Only if draining or for protection 只需在有分泌物或需保護時覆蓋 |
| **Is bruising normal? 瘀青正常嗎？** | Yes, mild bruising near the site is common 是的，傷口附近輕微瘀青是常見的 |
| **When will the scar fade? 疤痕何時會淡化？** | Scars continue to fade for 1-2 years 疤痕會持續淡化1-2年 |
