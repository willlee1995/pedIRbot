# When to Seek Help: Emergency Guide for Parents
# 何時尋求幫助：家長緊急指南

**Source 資料來源:** SickKids, CHOP, Boston Children's Hospital
**Category 類別:** Complication Management 併發症處理
**Language 語言:** Bilingual English/Traditional Chinese 中英雙語

---

## Overview 概述

After your child's interventional radiology procedure, most recovery is smooth and uneventful. However, it's important to know when something needs medical attention. This guide helps you recognize warning signs and know what action to take.

孩子接受介入放射治療後，大多數康復過程順利。然而，了解何時需要醫療關注非常重要。本指南幫助您識別警號並知道應採取的行動。

---

## Quick Reference: When to Act 快速參考：何時行動

| Color 顏色 | Action Level 行動級別 | Examples 例子 |
|------------|----------------------|---------------|
| 🟢 **GREEN 綠色** | Normal - Continue home care 正常 - 繼續家居護理 | Mild bruising, mild soreness 輕微瘀青、輕微酸痛 |
| 🟡 **YELLOW 黃色** | Call doctor during office hours 在辦公時間致電醫生 | Low-grade fever, increasing redness 低燒、紅腫增加 |
| 🟠 **ORANGE 橙色** | Call doctor urgently (same day) 緊急致電醫生（當天） | Moderate bleeding, high fever 中度出血、高燒 |
| 🔴 **RED 紅色** | Emergency - Call 999 or go to A&E 緊急 - 致電999或到急症室 | Severe bleeding, breathing problems 嚴重出血、呼吸困難 |

## 🔴 EMERGENCY: Call 999 or Go to A&E Immediately 緊急：立即致電999或到急症室

### These require immediate emergency care 以下情況需要立即緊急護理

#### Breathing Problems 呼吸問題
- ❌ Difficulty breathing 呼吸困難
- ❌ Rapid or shallow breathing 呼吸急促或淺
- ❌ Blue lips or fingernails 嘴唇或指甲發藍
- ❌ Wheezing or stridor (high-pitched breathing sound) 喘鳴或喘息（高音呼吸聲）

#### Severe Bleeding 嚴重出血
- ❌ Bleeding from the site that will not stop after 15 minutes of firm pressure 用力按壓15分鐘後穿刺部位仍持續出血
- ❌ Blood spurting or pulsating from the site 血液從穿刺部位噴出或搏動性出血
- ❌ Large expanding lump at the site 穿刺部位出現持續擴大的腫塊
- ❌ Blood in stool or vomit (may indicate internal bleeding) 大便或嘔吐物中有血（可能表示內出血）

#### Neurological Concerns 神經系統問題
- ❌ Seizures 抽搐
- ❌ Difficulty waking your child 難以叫醒孩子
- ❌ Confusion or not recognizing you 困惑或不認識您
- ❌ Weakness on one side of body 身體一側無力
- ❌ Severe sudden headache (after brain/head procedures) 突發劇烈頭痛（腦部/頭部手術後）

#### Allergic Reaction 過敏反應
- ❌ Swelling of face, lips, or throat 面部、嘴唇或喉嚨腫脹
- ❌ Hives spreading over body 蕁麻疹遍布全身
- ❌ Difficulty swallowing 吞嚥困難

#### Circulation Problems 血液循環問題
- ❌ Limb used for procedure is cold, pale, or blue 用於手術的肢體冰冷、蒼白或發藍
- ❌ Cannot move fingers or toes on that limb 該肢體的手指或腳趾無法活動
- ❌ Severe pain in that limb 該肢體劇烈疼痛

## 🟠 URGENT: Call Doctor Same Day 緊急：當天致電醫生

### These need attention within hours 以下情況需要數小時內處理

#### Fever 發燒
| Symptom 症狀 | Action 行動 |
|--------------|-------------|
| Fever ≥38.5°C (101.3°F) 發燒≥38.5°C | Call doctor same day 當天致電醫生 |
| Fever with shaking/chills 發燒伴有發抖/寒顫 | Urgent call 緊急致電 |
| Fever ≥39°C (102.2°F) 發燒≥39°C | Seek immediate care 立即尋求護理 |

#### Bleeding Concerns 出血問題
- ⚠️ Moderate bleeding that stops with pressure 按壓後可止住的中度出血
- ⚠️ Blood soaking through the bandage 血液浸透繃帶
- ⚠️ Bruising that is rapidly expanding 快速擴大的瘀青

#### Vomiting 嘔吐
- ⚠️ Vomiting more than twice 嘔吐超過兩次
- ⚠️ Cannot keep any fluids down 無法保持任何液體不吐
- ⚠️ Vomiting blood or dark material 嘔吐血液或深色物質

#### Pain 疼痛
- ⚠️ Pain not controlled by prescribed medication 處方藥物無法控制的疼痛
- ⚠️ Pain getting significantly worse 疼痛明顯惡化
- ⚠️ New pain in a different area 其他部位出現新疼痛

## 🟡 CALL DURING OFFICE HOURS 在辦公時間致電

### These need attention but can wait for next working day 需要關注但可等到下一個工作天

#### Site Concerns 穿刺部位問題
- 📞 Mild redness around the site 穿刺部位周圍輕微發紅
- 📞 Minor oozing from the site 穿刺部位輕微滲液
- 📞 Small hard lump at the site 穿刺部位小硬塊
- 📞 Stitches coming loose 縫線鬆脫

#### General Concerns 一般問題
- 📞 Low-grade fever (37.5-38°C) 低燒（37.5-38°C）
- 📞 Mild nausea persisting beyond 24 hours 持續超過24小時的輕微噁心
- 📞 Questions about medications 關於藥物的問題
- 📞 Questions about activity restrictions 關於活動限制的問題
- 📞 Constipation (common after sedation) 便秘（鎮靜後常見）

## 🟢 NORMAL: Continue Home Care 正常：繼續家居護理

### These are expected and not cause for concern 以下情況屬正常，不必擔心

#### In the First 24-48 Hours 首24-48小時內
- ✅ Mild bruising near the procedure site 穿刺部位附近輕微瘀青
- ✅ Mild soreness at the site 穿刺部位輕微酸痛
- ✅ Drowsiness from anesthesia/sedation 因麻醉/鎮靜而睏倦
- ✅ Mild nausea (especially first few hours) 輕微噁心（尤其是首數小時）
- ✅ Sore throat if a breathing tube was used 如使用呼吸管會喉嚨痛
- ✅ Irritability or mild confusion when waking 甦醒時易怒或輕微困惑
- ✅ Small amount of swelling at the site 穿刺部位輕微腫脹

#### In the Following Days 之後數天
- ✅ Bruising changing colors (purple → green → yellow) 瘀青顏色變化（紫色→綠色→黃色）
- ✅ Mild itching as the site heals 傷口癒合時輕微痕癢
- ✅ Scab formation 結痂

## Specific Procedure Complications 特定手術併發症

### After Sclerotherapy 硬化療法後

| Symptom 症狀 | Normal 正常 | When to Call 何時致電 |
|--------------|-------------|----------------------|
| Swelling 腫脹 | Mild-moderate for 1-2 weeks 1-2周內輕中度腫脹 | Severe or rapidly increasing 嚴重或快速增加 |
| Pain 疼痛 | Expected, managed with medication 預期之中，可用藥物控制 | Not controlled by medication 藥物無法控制 |
| Skin changes 皮膚變化 | Mild bruising, temporary discoloration 輕微瘀青、暫時變色 | Blistering, ulceration 起水泡、潰瘍 |

### After Embolization 栓塞後

| Symptom 症狀 | Normal 正常 | When to Call 何時致電 |
|--------------|-------------|----------------------|
| Post-embolization syndrome 栓塞後綜合症 | Mild fever, fatigue, pain for 2-3 days 2-3天輕微發燒、疲勞、疼痛 | High fever, severe pain 高燒、劇烈疼痛 |
| Site bruising 穿刺部位瘀青 | Common 常見 | Expanding hematoma 持續擴大的血腫 |

### After Central Line/Port Placement 中央靜脈導管/植入式輸液港置入後

| Symptom 症狀 | When to Call 何時致電 |
|--------------|----------------------|
| Redness around the line 導管周圍發紅 | If spreading or worsening 如擴散或惡化 |
| Fever with chills 發燒伴有寒顫 | Anytime (possible line infection) 任何時間（可能是導管感染） |
| Swelling in arm/neck/face 手臂/頸部/面部腫脹 | Immediately (possible clot) 立即（可能是血栓） |

## How to Describe Symptoms 如何描述症狀

When calling, have this information ready:
致電時，請準備以下資訊：

| Information 資訊 | Example 例子 |
|------------------|--------------|
| Child's name and age 孩子姓名和年齡 | "Chan Siu Ming, 6 years old 陳小明，6歲" |
| Procedure date 手術日期 | "Embolization on January 15 1月15日栓塞手術" |
| Current symptom 目前症狀 | "Fever of 38.5°C since this morning 今早起發燒38.5°C" |
| When it started 何時開始 | "Started 2 hours ago 2小時前開始" |
| What you've tried 您嘗試過什麼 | "Gave Panadol 30 minutes ago 30分鐘前給了撲熱息痛" |
| How child looks now 孩子目前狀況 | "Alert but tired 清醒但疲倦" |
