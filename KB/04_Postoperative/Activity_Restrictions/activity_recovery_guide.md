# Post-Procedure Care: Activity and Recovery
# 術後護理：活動與康復

**Source 資料來源:** Boston Children's Hospital, CHOP, SickKids
**Category 類別:** Postoperative Advice 術後建議
**Language 語言:** Bilingual English/Traditional Chinese 中英雙語

---

## Overview 概述

After your child's interventional radiology procedure, following proper recovery guidelines helps ensure healing and prevents complications. Most IR procedures allow same-day discharge with careful home care.

孩子接受介入放射治療後，遵循適當的康復指引有助確保傷口癒合並預防併發症。大多數介入放射治療可在當日出院，但需要仔細的家居護理。

---

## Immediate Recovery (First 24 Hours) 即時恢復（首24小時）

### Activity Level 活動水平

| Time 時間 | Activity 活動 | Notes 備註 |
|-----------|---------------|------------|
| Rest of procedure day 手術當天餘下時間 | Rest quietly at home 在家安靜休息 | No school, no daycare 不上學，不去托兒所 |
| First night 第一晚 | Normal sleep, check on child periodically 正常睡眠，定時查看孩子 | Wake for scheduled medications if needed 如需要，按時叫醒服藥 |
| Next morning 翌日早上 | Light activity okay 可進行輕度活動 | Usually can return to school/daycare 通常可返回學校/托兒所 |

### For Groin Punctures (Femoral Access) 腹股溝穿刺（股動脈通道）

If the procedure accessed blood vessels through the groin:
如手術從腹股溝進入血管：

- Keep leg straight for first 2-4 hours as instructed 按指示保持腿部伸直首2-4小時
- Avoid running or jumping for 24-48 hours 避免跑跳24-48小時
- No bike riding for 48-72 hours 48-72小時內不可騎單車

---

## Activity Restrictions 活動限制

### First Week (7 Days) 首周（7天）

Avoid these activities:
避免以下活動：

| Activity 活動 | Reason 原因 |
|---------------|-------------|
| ❌ Strenuous exercise 劇烈運動 | May cause bleeding at site 可能導致穿刺部位出血 |
| ❌ Heavy lifting 提舉重物 | Strains procedure area 對手術區域造成壓力 |
| ❌ Contact sports 接觸性運動 | Risk of injury to site 穿刺部位有受傷風險 |
| ❌ Swimming 游泳 | Infection risk until site heals 傷口未癒合前有感染風險 |
| ❌ Gym/PE class 體育課 | Wait for doctor's clearance 需待醫生批准 |
| ❌ Vigorous running 劇烈跑步 | Increases bleeding risk 增加出血風險 |

### Activities Usually Allowed 通常可進行的活動

| Activity 活動 | When 何時 |
|---------------|-----------|
| ✅ Walking 步行 | Next day 翌日 |
| ✅ Quiet play 安靜玩耍 | Immediately 即時 |
| ✅ Reading, watching TV 閱讀、看電視 | Immediately 即時 |
| ✅ Video games 電子遊戲 | Immediately 即時 |
| ✅ Light schoolwork 輕度功課 | Next day 翌日 |
| ✅ Going outdoors 外出 | Next day (avoid direct sun on site) 翌日（避免陽光直射傷口） |

## Diet After Procedure 術後飲食

### First Few Hours 首數小時

| Step 步驟 | Instructions 指示 |
|-----------|------------------|
| **Start with:** 開始時 | Clear liquids (water, apple juice) 清澈液體（清水、蘋果汁） |
| **If no vomiting:** 如無嘔吐 | Progress to light snacks 可進食輕食 |
| **Then:** 然後 | Return to normal diet 恢復正常飲食 |

### Hydration 補充水分

> 💧 **Important 重要:** Encourage plenty of fluids! This helps flush out any contrast dye used during the procedure.
> 鼓勵多飲水！這有助排出手術期間使用的造影劑。

**Recommended fluids 建議飲品:**
- Water 清水
- Diluted juice 稀釋果汁
- Electrolyte drinks (like Pedialyte for younger children) 電解質飲品（如兒童電解質補充液）

## Pain Management 疼痛管理

### Expected Discomfort 預期不適

After most IR procedures, discomfort is usually:
大多數介入放射治療後，不適通常是：

- Mild to moderate 輕度至中度
- Located at the procedure site 位於手術部位
- Manageable at home with simple medications 可用簡單藥物在家處理

### Medications 藥物

| Medication 藥物 | Use 用途 | Notes 備註 |
|-----------------|----------|------------|
| **Acetaminophen 撲熱息痛** (Panadol/Tylenol) | First-line pain relief 一線止痛藥 | Follow dosing by weight 按體重服用 |
| **Ibuprofen 布洛芬** (Nurofen/Advil) | Alternative or add-on 替代或附加藥物 | Take with food 與食物同服 |

> ⚠️ **Important 重要:** Follow the dosing instructions provided by your healthcare team. Do not give aspirin to children.
> 請遵循醫療團隊提供的劑量指示。不要給兒童服用阿司匹林。

### Pain Red Flags 疼痛警號

Contact your doctor if:
如有以下情況請聯繫醫生：

- Pain is severe 劇烈疼痛
- Pain gets worse instead of better 疼痛惡化而非好轉
- Pain medication doesn't help 止痛藥無效
- New pain develops in a different area 其他部位出現新疼痛

## Recovery Timeline 康復時間表

| Time 時間 | Milestone 里程碑 |
|-----------|------------------|
| **Day 0 (Procedure day) 第0天（手術當天）** | Rest, light fluids, pain medication as needed 休息、飲用清澈液體、按需服用止痛藥 |
| **Day 1 翌日** | Dressing may be removed, shower okay, return to light activity 可移除敷料、可淋浴、恢復輕度活動 |
| **Day 2-3 第2-3天** | Return to school/daycare likely, continue activity restrictions 可能可返回學校/托兒所，繼續限制活動 |
| **Day 7 第7天** | Most activity restrictions lifted 大部分活動限制解除 |
| **Day 7-14 第7-14天** | Full return to normal activities including sports 完全恢復正常活動包括運動 |

> 📝 **Note 備註:** Your doctor will provide specific guidance based on the type of procedure your child had.
> 醫生會根據孩子接受的手術類型提供具體指引。

## Sleep and Rest 睡眠與休息

### First Night 第一晚

- Your child may be more tired than usual from anesthesia/sedation 孩子可能因麻醉/鎮靜而比平常更疲倦
- Normal sleep is encouraged 鼓勵正常睡眠
- Check on your child periodically 定期查看孩子
- Keep prescribed medications on schedule 按時服用處方藥物

### Following Days 之後數天

- Extra rest helps healing 額外休息有助康復
- Let your child's energy level guide activity 讓孩子的精力水平引導活動
- Don't push too hard too fast 不要操之過急

## School and Childcare 學校與托兒

### Return Timeline 返回時間

| Procedure Type 手術類型 | Typical Return 一般返回時間 |
|-------------------------|----------------------------|
| Simple puncture procedures 簡單穿刺手術 | Next day 翌日 |
| Sclerotherapy 硬化療法 | 2-3 days 2-3天 |
| More complex procedures 較複雜手術 | As advised by doctor 按醫生建議 |

### What to Tell the School 告知學校的事項

Provide a note from the hospital that includes:
提供包含以下內容的醫院證明：

- Child cannot participate in PE/sports 孩子不能參加體育/運動
- Duration of activity restriction 活動限制的時期
- Any special accommodations needed 任何所需的特別安排

## Follow-Up Appointments 覆診預約

### What to Expect 預期情況

- Most procedures have a follow-up appointment scheduled 大多數手術已安排覆診預約
- This may be a clinic visit or phone call 可能是診所探訪或電話跟進
- Sometimes imaging (ultrasound, CT) is needed at follow-up 有時覆診時需要影像檢查（超聲波、CT）

### Before Your Appointment 覆診前

Keep track of:
記錄以下事項：

- Any symptoms or concerns 任何症狀或疑慮
- Pain levels and medication use 疼痛程度和藥物使用
- How your child is recovering 孩子的康復情況
- Questions for the medical team 向醫療團隊提問的問題
