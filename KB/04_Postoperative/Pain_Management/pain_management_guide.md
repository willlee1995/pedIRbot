# Pain Management After Procedures
# 術後疼痛管理

**Source 資料來源:** Boston Children's Hospital, CHOP, KidsHealth
**Category 類別:** Postoperative Advice 術後建議
**Language 語言:** Bilingual English/Traditional Chinese 中英雙語

---

## Overview 概述

Some discomfort after an interventional radiology procedure is normal. Proper pain management helps your child recover comfortably and return to normal activities.

介入放射治療後有些不適是正常的。適當的疼痛管理有助孩子舒適康復並恢復正常活動。

---

## What to Expect 預期情況

### Normal Post-Procedure Discomfort 正常術後不適

| Location 位置 | Description 描述 | Duration 持續時間 |
|---------------|------------------|-------------------|
| Procedure site 手術部位 | Tenderness, mild soreness 觸痛、輕微酸痛 | 1-3 days 1-3天 |
| IV site 靜脈注射部位 | Mild bruising, slight soreness 輕微瘀青、輕微酸痛 | 2-4 days 2-4天 |
| Throat 喉嚨 | Soreness if breathing tube was used 如使用呼吸管會酸痛 | 1-2 days 1-2天 |
| General 全身 | Mild body aches 輕微全身酸痛 | 1-2 days 1-2天 |

## Pain Medications 止痛藥物

### First Choice: Acetaminophen 首選：撲熱息痛

**Brand names 品牌名稱:** Panadol, Tylenol

| Age Group 年齡組別 | Form 劑型 |
|-------------------|-----------|
| Infants 嬰兒 | Drops or liquid 滴劑或液體 |
| Toddlers 幼童 | Liquid/suspension 液體/懸浮液 |
| Older children 較大兒童 | Chewable tablets or tablets 咀嚼片或藥片 |

**Key Points 要點:**
- ✅ Safe for most children 對大多數兒童安全
- ✅ Can be given on an empty stomach 可空腹服用
- ✅ Dose based on weight, not age 劑量根據體重而非年齡
- ⚠️ Follow package instructions or doctor's guidance 遵循包裝說明或醫生指引
- ⚠️ Do not exceed maximum daily dose 不要超過每日最大劑量

---

### Alternative: Ibuprofen 替代：布洛芬

**Brand names 品牌名稱:** Nurofen, Advil, Motrin

| Age Group 年齡組別 | Form 劑型 | Notes 備註 |
|-------------------|-----------|------------|
| Children 6+ months 6個月以上兒童 | Liquid/suspension 液體/懸浮液 | Give with food 與食物同服 |
| Older children 較大兒童 | Tablets 藥片 | Give with food 與食物同服 |

**Key Points 要點:**
- ✅ Also reduces inflammation 也可減輕炎症
- ✅ May be more effective for some types of pain 對某些疼痛可能更有效
- ⚠️ Give with food to prevent stomach upset 與食物同服以防胃部不適
- ⚠️ Not for children under 6 months 不適用於6個月以下嬰兒
- ⚠️ Avoid if kidney problems or dehydration 如有腎臟問題或脫水應避免使用

---

### Alternating Medications 交替使用藥物

Some doctors recommend alternating between acetaminophen and ibuprofen:
部分醫生建議交替使用撲熱息痛和布洛芬：

| Time 時間 | Medication 藥物 |
|-----------|-----------------|
| 12:00 noon 中午12:00 | Acetaminophen 撲熱息痛 |
| 4:00 PM 下午4:00 | Ibuprofen 布洛芬 |
| 8:00 PM 晚上8:00 | Acetaminophen 撲熱息痛 |
| 12:00 midnight 午夜12:00 | Ibuprofen 布洛芬 |

> ⚠️ **Important 重要:** Only alternate medications if instructed by your doctor. Keep a written log to avoid confusion and prevent accidental overdose.
> 只有在醫生指示下才交替使用藥物。保留書面記錄以避免混淆和意外過量。

---

### Medications to AVOID 應避免的藥物

| Medication 藥物 | Reason 原因 |
|-----------------|-------------|
| ❌ **Aspirin 阿司匹林** | Risk of Reye's syndrome in children 兒童有雷氏綜合症風險 |
| ❌ **Adult formulations 成人配方** | Too strong for children 對兒童太強 |
| ❌ **Codeine 可待因** | Not recommended for children in most cases 大多數情況下不建議兒童使用 |

## Non-Medication Pain Relief 非藥物止痛方法

### Ice Packs 冰敷

For bruising and swelling at the procedure site:
用於穿刺部位的瘀青和腫脹：

**How to use 使用方法:**
1. Wrap ice pack in a thin cloth 用薄布包裹冰袋
2. Apply to the area for 15-20 minutes 敷在該區域15-20分鐘
3. Remove for at least 20 minutes 移除至少20分鐘
4. Repeat as needed 按需重複

> ⚠️ Never apply ice directly to skin 切勿將冰直接敷在皮膚上

## Position for Comfort 舒適體位

### Lying Position 臥位

- Pillows under knees can reduce strain 膝下放枕頭可減輕壓力
- Elevating the procedure limb (if applicable) may reduce swelling 抬高手術肢體（如適用）可減輕腫脹
- Side-lying may be more comfortable than lying flat 側臥可能比平躺更舒適

### Sitting Position 坐位

- Use pillows for support 使用枕頭支撐
- Keep procedure area protected 保護手術區域
- Change positions regularly to prevent stiffness 定時轉換姿勢以防僵硬

## When Pain is a Warning Sign 當疼痛是警號時

### Seek Medical Care If 如有以下情況請尋求醫療護理

| Warning Sign 警號 | Action 行動 |
|-------------------|-------------|
| Pain gets worse instead of better 疼痛惡化而非好轉 | Call doctor 致電醫生 |
| Pain medication doesn't help 止痛藥無效 | Call doctor 致電醫生 |
| Severe pain (8-10 on scale) 劇烈疼痛（量表8-10） | Seek urgent care 尋求緊急護理 |
| Pain with fever 疼痛伴有發燒 | Call doctor 致電醫生 |
| Pain with swelling/redness 疼痛伴有腫脹/發紅 | Call doctor 致電醫生 |
| New pain in different location 其他位置出現新疼痛 | Call doctor 致電醫生 |

## Tips for Giving Medication 服藥小貼士

### For Liquid Medications 液體藥物

- Use the measuring device provided (syringe, cup) 使用提供的量度器具（注射器、量杯）
- Aim for the inside of the cheek 對準面頰內側
- Give slowly to prevent choking 慢慢給予以防嗆到
- Follow with a small drink if allowed 如可以，之後喝少量飲品

### For Tablets 藥片

- Check if tablets can be crushed (not all can) 檢查藥片是否可壓碎（並非全部可以）
- Chewable tablets are easier for young children 咀嚼片對幼童更容易
- Some tablets can be hidden in food (e.g., yogurt) 部分藥片可藏在食物中（如乳酪）

### Timing Tips 時間小貼士

- Give pain medication on schedule for first 24-48 hours 首24-48小時按時給予止痛藥
- Don't wait until pain is severe 不要等到疼痛劇烈
- Set phone alarms to remember doses 設置手機鬧鐘提醒劑量
